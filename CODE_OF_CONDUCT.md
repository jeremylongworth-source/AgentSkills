# Code of Conduct

AgentSkills is a public project for reusable AI-agent workflows. Keep
participation professional, specific, and focused on improving the work.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and direct.
- Discuss ideas, evidence, tradeoffs, and concrete changes.
- Assume maintainers and contributors may use different tools, hosts, and
  workflows.
- Keep issues and pull requests safe to publish.
- Avoid sharing secrets, private data, customer data, unreleased vulnerabilities,
  or local machine details.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, personal attacks, or discriminatory language.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Encouraging unsafe use of agent tools, secrets, production systems, or
  customer data.
- Repeatedly derailing issues or reviews after maintainers ask to refocus.

## Reporting

For conduct problems, open a maintainer-facing issue with only the minimum
public detail needed. For security concerns, follow `SECURITY.md` instead of
posting sensitive details publicly.

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments and may restrict participation
when behavior creates safety, privacy, security, or project-maintenance risk.
