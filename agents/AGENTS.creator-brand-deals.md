Use local skills as the primary routing layer for creator brand-deal work.

Use `brand-pitch` for creator sponsor outreach, partnership angles, intro
messages, and follow-up notes.

Use `media-kit-builder` for media kit copy, creator one-sheets, sponsor
profiles, partnership pages, audience summaries, and proof tables.

Use `sponsorship-package` for sponsorship tiers, deliverable menus, package
options, campaign add-ons, reporting inclusions, exclusions, and package
framing.

Use `rate-card-builder` for draft creator rate cards, package pricing
structures, usage-rights adjustments, add-ons, assumptions, and missing input
lists.

Use `campaign-proposal` for sponsor campaign concepts, scopes, deliverable
plans, timelines, approvals, measurement plans, assumptions, and next steps.

Use `usage-rights-checklist` for paid usage, whitelisting, licensing,
exclusivity, term length, territory, derivative-use, approval, and contract
review questions.

Use `deal-negotiation-prep` for counteroffer notes, tradeoff tables,
must-haves, objection responses, red flags, and review checklists.

Use `deliverables-tracker` for campaign status tables, due dates, approval
owners, asset links, reporting dates, blockers, and next actions.

Use `campaign-recap` for sponsor recap reports, delivered assets, source-backed
metrics, audience response, learnings, caveats, and recommendations.

Use `renewal-pitch` for follow-up sponsor pitches, next-campaign angles,
renewal emails, measurement plans, and approval notes.

Use `caption-writer` for sponsor-safe captions and disclosure notes,
`marketing-positioning-strategy` for fit and positioning,
`sales-conversion-enablement` for reviewed conversion assets, and
`concise-technical-writing` for tighter sponsor-facing copy.

Do not send sponsor outreach, accept terms, approve contracts, commit pricing,
publish sponsored content, or represent legal, tax, financial, or platform
advice as final guidance. Verify current platform, disclosure, and paid-media
rules when exact tactical guidance matters.
