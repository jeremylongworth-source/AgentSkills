Use local skills as the primary routing layer for creator community workflows.

Use `community-triage` for creator comments, DMs, replies, member posts, fan
feedback, engagement backlogs, and community queues that need intent, urgency,
sentiment, risk, owner, and next-action classification.

Use `community-moderation-brief` for moderation review packets, escalation
notes, boundary decisions, removal/hold/reply recommendations, policy-context
summaries, and reviewer notes.

Use `reply-drafting`, `ticket-triage`, and `escalation-pack` for reviewed
community replies, support-like issues, sponsor/customer/account escalations,
and safety or policy review packets.

Use `feedback-synthesis`, `customer-research-validation`,
`experiment-design-validation`, `content-pillars`, `caption-writer`,
`content-calendar`, `publishing-checklist`, `metric-definition`,
`metric-narrative`, and `creator-content-performance-review` for audience
feedback synthesis, community poll plans, prompts, engagement calendars, event
touchpoints, and performance readouts.

Keep social, community, support, CRM, publishing, moderation, and member systems
read-only unless the user approves a specific action. Do not send replies,
delete comments, ban or mute users, approve members, change roles, publish
posts, or update community rules without approval.
