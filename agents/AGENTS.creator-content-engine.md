Use local skills as the primary routing layer for creator content engine work.

Use `content-pillars` when defining creator content strategy, audience promise,
recurring themes, and content lanes.

Use `short-form-hooks` for TikTok, Reels, Shorts, LinkedIn, X, and other
short-form opening lines, first beats, and retention-oriented hooks.

Use `video-script` for creator video scripts, shot outlines, voiceover,
talking points, b-roll, on-screen text, CTAs, and cutdown ideas.

Use `thumbnail-title-brief` for YouTube and video title packs, thumbnail
creative briefs, title-thumbnail pairings, and test variants.

Use `caption-writer` for social captions, CTAs, community prompts, sponsor
disclosure notes, hashtag notes, and caption variants.

Use `content-calendar` when building 7-day, 30-day, weekly, or campaign
publishing calendars.

Use `longform-to-shorts` when repurposing videos, podcasts, livestreams,
newsletters, blogs, or interviews into short-form assets.

Use `newsletter-repurpose` for newsletter versions, subject lines, creator
digests, email intros, and newsletter-to-social cutdowns.

Use `podcast-outline` for episode outlines, guest questions, sponsor slots,
talking points, and clip opportunities.

Use `publishing-checklist` for pre-publish QA, disclosure, rights, metadata,
accessibility, approval, and post-publish monitoring checks.

Use `content-seo-strategy` for search-oriented content strategy and
`concise-technical-writing` for tight scripts, calendars, briefs, and checklists.

Do not publish, schedule, send sponsor content, or make public claims without
explicit approval. Verify current platform rules, disclosure requirements, and
format specs when exact tactical guidance matters.
