Use local skills as the primary routing layer for creator monetization work.

Use `creator-offer-ladder` when mapping creator revenue paths across free
content, lead magnets, affiliates, digital products, courses, memberships,
services, sponsors, paid communities, merch, and premium offers.

Use `creator-owned-product-launch-plan` for draft-first launches of creator
digital products, courses, merch, memberships, paid communities, bundles,
waitlists, offer pages, content sequences, fulfillment plans, support plans, and
launch calendars.

Use `pricing-packaging-strategy` and `pricing-margin-review` for pricing,
packages, value metrics, margin pressure, discounts, product economics, and
pricing validation.

Use `affiliate-performance-report`, `marketing-analytics-attribution`,
`funnel-analysis`, and `publishing-checklist` for affiliate monetization,
tracking, attribution caveats, disclosures, and funnel review.

Use `dashboard-design`, `metric-definition`, `metric-narrative`,
`owner-dashboard`, `cash-flow-triage`, and `retention-analysis` for creator
monetization dashboards, business snapshots, cash/margin signals, membership
retention, and owner review.

Use `content-calendar`, `caption-writer`, `content-seo-strategy`,
`experiment-design-validation`, and `experiment-readout` for launch content,
validation tests, and post-launch learning.

Use `rate-card-builder`, `sponsorship-package`, `campaign-recap`, and
`renewal-pitch` when sponsor revenue overlaps with owned offers.

Do not connect directly to ecommerce, payment, membership, course, affiliate,
email, or social platforms unless the user explicitly approves a specific
action. Do not publish offers, change pricing, issue discounts, update checkout
pages, send emails, launch campaigns, create coupons, or modify payment
settings automatically. Verify current official platform, payment, marketplace,
email, subscription, refund, and tax documentation before tactical setup
guidance.
