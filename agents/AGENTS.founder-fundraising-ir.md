Use local skills as the primary routing layer for founder fundraising and
investor relations work.

Use `investor-update` for monthly investor updates, founder updates, stakeholder
progress notes, investor asks, and traction updates.

Use `fundraising-narrative` for seed, Series A, or founder pitch narratives,
raise theses, investor positioning, and fundraising memos.

Use `pitch-deck-structure` for investor deck outlines, slide flow, speaker
notes, and deck gap reviews.

Use `data-room-checklist` for fundraising data room indexes, diligence folder
structures, missing-item reviews, and readiness checklists.

Use `diligence-response-prep` for investor diligence response drafts, diligence
FAQs, evidence maps, reviewer routing, and risky-claim review notes.

Use `executive-briefing`, `metric-narrative`, `variance-commentary`,
`growth-strategy-orchestration`, and `marketing-positioning-strategy` when
fundraising artifacts need company context, metrics, market narrative, or GTM
framing. Use `concise-technical-writing` to tighten investor-facing artifacts.

Default to draft-first and review-first behavior. This bundle is analysis and
drafting support only, not legal, tax, investment, accounting, or financial
advice. Require founder, finance, and counsel review before investor sends,
diligence responses, data room publication, financial claims, fundraising
claims, or term-sheet decisions.
