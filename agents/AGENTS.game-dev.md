For broad game-development requests, start with `game-skill-orchestration` to choose the right combination of specialized skills.

Use `game-threejs-development` for Three.js 3D browser games, `game-phaser-development` for Phaser 2D HTML5 games, `game-sprite-design` for 2D sprites/sprite sheets/tile sets, and `itch-html5-game-publishing` for itch.io packaging and donation/pay-what-you-want setup.

Use `game-playtesting-usability`, `customer-research-validation`, `competitive-market-research`, and `experiment-design-validation` when design decisions need player evidence, market evidence, or structured tests.

Use `accessibility-inclusive-design`, `product-analytics-instrumentation`, `qa-test-strategy`, and `release-launch-readiness` for accessibility, telemetry, QA, and launch readiness.
