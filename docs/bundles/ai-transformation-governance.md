# AI Transformation Governance Bundle Brief

## Problem

Executives and AI transformation leads need agents to turn AI enthusiasm into a
governed portfolio of use cases, pilots, risks, vendors, adoption plans, and
pilot-to-scale roadmaps without creating uncontrolled production commitments.

## Target User

CEOs, CIOs, CTOs, AI transformation leads, chiefs of staff, product leaders,
security/privacy reviewers, and operators responsible for AI adoption.

## Included Skills

- `ai-use-case-portfolio`: map and score AI opportunities.
- `ai-pilot-selection`: choose pilots with hypotheses, metrics, guardrails, and
  decision rules.
- `ai-risk-register`: identify AI risks, controls, owners, and escalation paths.
- `ai-vendor-evaluation`: compare vendors, evidence, procurement questions, and
  review needs.
- `ai-governance-checklist`: define governance domains, owners, evidence, and
  gates.
- `ai-adoption-plan`: plan rollout, training, support, communication, and
  adoption metrics.
- `pilot-to-scale-roadmap`: turn pilot results into governed rollout decisions.
- `experiment-design-validation`, `product-analytics-instrumentation`, and
  `skill-evaluation-iteration`: support metrics, telemetry, and evaluation.
- `concise-technical-writing`: tighten executive artifacts.

## Context Files

- `AI_STRATEGY.md`: business goals, AI principles, target functions, and
  constraints.
- `AI_USE_CASE_PORTFOLIO.md`: candidate use cases, scores, owners, and status.
- `AI_GOVERNANCE_POLICY.md`: review gates, autonomy limits, data rules, and
  owners.
- `AI_RISK_REGISTER.md`: AI risks, controls, owners, and review cadence.
- `AI_PILOT_SCORECARD.md`: pilot hypotheses, metrics, guardrails, and decision
  rules.
- `AI_ADOPTION_PLAN.md`: rollout phases, training, support, and feedback loops.

## MCP Preset Intent

Use documentation research for current public docs, vendor materials, platform
behavior, and governance references. Keep business systems, data warehouses,
identity/admin systems, security tools, and production AI systems read-only
unless explicit owner approval is given.

## Safety Rules

- Governance-first and review-first by default.
- Do not approve production AI rollout, vendor commitments, sensitive data
  access, autonomous actions, policy changes, or employee-impacting decisions.
- Do not invent ROI, adoption, vendor capability, compliance status, security
  posture, or data readiness.
- Verify current official documentation before platform-specific vendor,
  security, privacy, or compliance guidance.
- Require legal, security, privacy, people, finance, or executive review when
  risk touches those domains.

## Pilot Metrics

- Operational: time to portfolio shortlist or pilot scorecard.
- Quality: governance gap count and reviewer correction rate.
- Adoption: pilot owner acceptance and training completion.
- Economic: percentage of pilots with clear continue/revise/scale/stop decision.

## Acceptance Criteria

- The bundle installs with a dry run.
- Use-case portfolio, pilot selection, governance, vendor, adoption, and
  pilot-to-scale scenarios route to the intended skills.
- Outputs separate strategy, evidence, assumptions, governance gaps, and owner
  approvals.
- Production AI, sensitive data, vendor, policy, and employee-impacting actions
  require explicit review.
