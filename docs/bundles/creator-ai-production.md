# Creator AI Production Bundle Brief

## Problem

Creators increasingly use AI for images, video concepts, b-roll, thumbnails,
voiceover drafts, editing ideas, synthetic personas, and batch production. The
workflow is useful only if it preserves audience trust, source provenance,
rights review, disclosure, human review, and platform/vendor neutrality. This
bundle creates production planning artifacts, not a generation platform.

## Target User

Influencers, creator-founders, editors, creative producers, assistants,
creator managers, agencies, and creator teams using AI-assisted production.

## Included Skills

- `ai-media-prompt-brief`: prepare provider-neutral image, video, audio,
  voiceover, thumbnail, b-roll, and batch media prompt briefs with creative
  intent, source references, constraints, exclusions, rights/provenance notes,
  acceptance criteria, and review gates.
- `ai-video-prompt-pack`: turn video concepts into model-aware prompt packs
  with scene timing, safe areas, variants, negative prompts, and regeneration
  notes for tools such as Gemini/Veo, Sora, Runway, Pika, or Luma.
- `synthetic-character-bible`: define synthetic personas, virtual influencers,
  recurring fictional hosts, mascots, disclosure rules, identity boundaries,
  voice/visual continuity, and prohibited claims.
- `creator-reputation-risk-review`: review AI labeling, brand safety,
  platform-policy uncertainty, public trust, and hold/revise/go decisions.
- `publishing-checklist` and `usage-rights-checklist`: check disclosure,
  rights, copyright, licensing, third-party assets, and pre-publish gates.
- `ai-risk-register` and `ai-governance-checklist`: track AI risk, human review,
  privacy, consent, provenance, and governance controls.
- `video-script`, `short-form-hooks`, `thumbnail-title-brief`,
  `social-asset-production`, `caption-writer`, `podcast-outline`, and
  `content-calendar`: support scripts, hooks, thumbnails, social visuals,
  captions, voiceover/podcast structures, and publishing plans.
- `creator-production-ops-brief`: connect AI production to asset handoffs,
  production dependencies, contractors, and approval flow.
- `concise-technical-writing`: tighten briefs, prompt packs, and reviewer notes.

## Context Files

- `AI_PRODUCTION_BRIEF.md`: concept, assets, prompt briefs, batch plan, review
  gates, owners, and delivery notes.
- `PROMPT_PACK.md`: provider-neutral image, video, audio, b-roll, thumbnail, and
  voiceover prompt briefs with negative constraints.
- `SOURCE_ASSETS.md`: source files, permissions, owners, restrictions, and
  provenance notes.
- `SYNTHETIC_CHARACTER_BIBLE.md`: character identity, role, voice, visual
  continuity, disclosure, prohibited claims, and review gates.
- `DISCLOSURE_AND_PROVENANCE.md`: AI assistance, synthetic media, sponsor,
  rights, source, and labeling notes.
- `HUMAN_REVIEW.md`: reviewer owners, approval state, missing facts, hold/revise
  notes, and escalation decisions.

## MCP Preset Intent

Use documentation research only when current official provider, platform,
commercial-use, AI-labeling, likeness, voice, disclosure, or content-policy
rules materially affect the plan. Keep generation providers, social platforms,
publishing tools, storage, sponsor systems, and production tools read-only
unless the user explicitly approves a specific action.

## Safety Rules

- This is production planning support only, not legal, copyright, licensing,
  privacy, platform-policy, commercial-use, or rights-clearance advice.
- Do not generate, publish, schedule, upload, send, report, or contact sponsors,
  platforms, or vendors without explicit approval.
- Do not claim legal clearance, copyright clearance, platform compliance,
  disclosure sufficiency, likeness consent, voice rights, sponsor approval, or
  commercial-use permission.
- Do not prompt for living artists, private people, protected characters,
  copyrighted works, voice clones, likeness replicas, logos, or confidential
  source assets without rights and review.
- Do not fabricate source assets, provenance, consent, tool settings, audience
  reaction, sponsor approval, or human authorship.
- Verify current official provider or platform guidance before tactical
  generation, labeling, commercial-use, or distribution advice.

## Pilot Metrics

- Operational: time from concept to AI production brief or prompt pack.
- Quality: creator/editor edit rate on prompt packs and synthetic character
  bibles.
- Adoption: percent of AI-assisted content work that uses provenance and review
  gates.
- Trust: reduction in missed disclosure, rights, likeness, voice, or platform
  checks.

## Acceptance Criteria

- The bundle installs with a dry run.
- AI content planning, image prompt, video prompt pack, synthetic character,
  voiceover, batch workflow, and authenticity review scenarios route to intended
  skills.
- Outputs include concrete artifacts: AI production brief, prompt pack,
  synthetic character bible, voiceover plan, source-asset checklist, provenance
  notes, human-review checklist, or hold/revise/go recommendation.
- Generation, publishing, rights, legal, platform-policy, sponsor, likeness,
  voice, and public-disclosure decisions remain human-reviewed.
