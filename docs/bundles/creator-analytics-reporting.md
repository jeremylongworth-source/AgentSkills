# Creator Analytics Reporting Bundle Brief

## Problem

Creators need to prove what worked across content, sponsors, affiliate links,
owned products, newsletters, and platforms. Generic analytics support can define
metrics, but creator reporting needs specific care around platform exports,
watch time, retention, sponsor deliverables, affiliate attribution, screenshots,
and claims that may be sent to brands or used in public positioning.

## Target User

Influencers, social creators, creator-founders, creator managers, agencies,
assistants, sponsor-facing operators, and small creator teams.

## Included Skills

- `creator-content-performance-review`: review supplied creator content
  analytics, watch time, retention, hooks, titles, thumbnails, and next content
  actions.
- `affiliate-performance-report`: report affiliate links, codes, conversions,
  commissions, attribution caveats, and creator commerce performance.
- `campaign-recap`: create sponsor campaign recaps from supplied links, metrics,
  screenshots, and learnings.
- `renewal-pitch`: turn reporting into a reviewed next-campaign ask.
- `deliverables-tracker`: track live links, proof assets, reporting dates, and
  recap requirements.
- `metric-definition`: define creator KPIs and source rules.
- `metric-narrative`: summarize metric movement and caveats.
- `dashboard-design`: design weekly, monthly, sponsor, or platform reporting
  views.
- `retention-analysis`, `cohort-analysis`, and `funnel-analysis`: support
  audience growth, subscriber retention, and creator funnel analysis.
- `experiment-readout` and `experiment-design-validation`: support content test
  readouts and future test design.
- `marketing-analytics-attribution`: handle UTMs, sponsor links, affiliate
  tracking, paid amplification, and attribution caveats.
- `analytics-instrumentation-plan`: define tracking plans, consent, QA, and
  link/event instrumentation.
- `concise-technical-writing`: tighten sponsor-facing reports and dashboard
  narratives.

## Context Files

- `CREATOR_METRICS.md`: canonical creator KPI definitions, source systems,
  date ranges, and owner.
- `PLATFORM_EXPORTS.md`: platform exports, screenshots, metric definitions,
  caveats, and refresh cadence.
- `SPONSOR_REPORTING.md`: sponsor objectives, deliverables, links, screenshots,
  reporting requirements, and review owner.
- `AFFILIATE_TRACKING.md`: links, codes, attribution windows, refunds, revenue
  or commission fields, and disclosure notes.
- `CONTENT_EXPERIMENTS.md`: hypotheses, variants, dates, metrics, guardrails,
  results, and decision rules.

## MCP Preset Intent

Use documentation research for current platform, affiliate, paid-media,
disclosure, and monetization rules when exact tactical details matter. Keep
platform, sponsor, affiliate, CRM, payment, analytics, and social tools
read-only unless the user explicitly approves a specific external action.

## Safety Rules

- Do not invent or inflate views, watch time, engagement, conversions, revenue,
  demographics, screenshots, testimonials, sponsor outcomes, or ROI.
- Separate observed metrics, source screenshots or exports, assumptions,
  attribution limits, platform caveats, and recommendations.
- Require human review before sending sponsor reports, renewal pitches, public
  claims, pricing commitments, investor or finance reporting, or monetization
  decisions.
- Treat attribution as directional unless source systems, UTM rules, affiliate
  windows, and tracking definitions are verified.
- Do not provide legal, tax, accounting, investment, or financial advice.
- Verify current platform, disclosure, affiliate, paid-media, and monetization
  rules when tactical specifics matter.

## Pilot Metrics

- Operational: time from platform exports to reviewed report or dashboard spec.
- Quality: reviewer edit rate on metrics, caveats, and recommendations.
- Adoption: percent of generated reports approved for sponsor or internal use.
- Economic: reduced reporting time and improved renewal-readiness.

## Acceptance Criteria

- The bundle installs with a dry run.
- Sponsor report, watch-time review, audience growth, affiliate report,
  experiment readout, and dashboard scenarios route to the intended skills.
- Outputs include concrete artifacts: sponsor performance report, content
  performance memo, audience growth review, affiliate report, experiment
  readout, dashboard spec, metric dictionary, or instrumentation plan.
- External sends, public claims, pricing commitments, and financial reporting
  require explicit human review.
