# Creator Brand Deals Bundle Brief

## Problem

Creators need a sponsorship workflow that turns audience trust into brand
partnerships without inflating metrics, hiding disclosures, or accepting risky
rights terms. Generic sales copy is not enough; the workflow must produce
media kits, pitches, packages, rate cards, proposals, trackers, recaps, and
renewal asks that can be reviewed before anything is sent or signed.

## Target User

Influencers, social creators, creator-founders, creator managers, agencies,
assistants, and small creator teams managing sponsorships or brand partnerships.

## Included Skills

- `brand-pitch`: draft sponsor outreach, partnership angles, and follow-ups.
- `media-kit-builder`: structure media kits, one-sheets, audience snapshots,
  proof tables, and missing asset lists.
- `sponsorship-package`: design deliverable bundles, package options, add-ons,
  reporting inclusions, and exclusions.
- `rate-card-builder`: draft rate cards, package pricing structures, usage
  adjustments, and pricing assumptions.
- `campaign-proposal`: create campaign concepts, deliverable plans, timelines,
  measurement plans, assumptions, and next steps.
- `usage-rights-checklist`: review paid usage, whitelisting, exclusivity,
  licensing, territory, term, derivative-use, and approval questions.
- `deal-negotiation-prep`: prepare counteroffers, tradeoffs, objection
  responses, red flags, and review checklists.
- `deliverables-tracker`: track sponsor deliverables, due dates, approval
  owners, links, reporting dates, blockers, and next actions.
- `campaign-recap`: create sponsor recap reports from supplied performance
  data, links, screenshots, and learnings.
- `renewal-pitch`: draft renewal angles, next-campaign proposals, follow-up
  emails, and measurement plans.
- `caption-writer`: support sponsor-safe captions and disclosures.
- `marketing-positioning-strategy`: support brand fit and positioning.
- `sales-conversion-enablement`: support reviewed conversion assets.
- `concise-technical-writing`: tighten sponsor-facing copy.

## Context Files

- `CREATOR_MEDIA_KIT.md`: positioning, audience, platforms, metrics, proof, and
  sponsor categories.
- `AUDIENCE_METRICS.md`: verified platform, email, affiliate, and campaign
  metrics with date ranges.
- `SPONSOR_POLICY.md`: accepted categories, avoid list, disclosure standards,
  usage-rights boundaries, and approval rules.
- `RATE_CARD.md`: current packages, draft rates, usage add-ons, exclusivity, and
  assumptions.
- `CAMPAIGN_TRACKER.md`: sponsor, deliverables, status, approvals, links,
  reporting, and renewal dates.
- `CONTRACT_REVIEW_NOTES.md`: terms, red flags, open questions, and professional
  review owner.

## MCP Preset Intent

Use documentation research for current platform, ad disclosure, paid-media,
email, and sponsorship rules when exact tactical details matter. Keep sponsor,
CRM, payment, contract, publishing, and analytics tools read-only unless the
user explicitly approves a specific external action.

## Safety Rules

- Do not fabricate or inflate audience metrics, sponsor logos, testimonials,
  case studies, conversion results, screenshots, or campaign outcomes.
- Do not send pitches, accept terms, publish sponsored content, invoice, or
  update sponsor systems without explicit approval.
- Do not provide legal, tax, accounting, investment, or financial advice.
- Require professional or manager review for contracts, licensing, exclusivity,
  whitelisting, paid usage, rights, claims, disclosures, and high-value deals.
- Verify current platform, disclosure, paid-media, affiliate, email, and
  monetization rules when tactical specifics matter.
- Avoid deceptive scarcity, hidden sponsorships, unsupported ROI claims, and
  promises of reach, virality, sales, conversions, or sponsor acceptance.

## Pilot Metrics

- Operational: time from inbound sponsor request to reviewed proposal.
- Quality: manager or creator edit rate on pitches, media kits, proposals, and
  recaps.
- Adoption: percent of generated sponsor assets approved for use after review.
- Economic: reduced deal-prep time and improved renewal readiness.

## Acceptance Criteria

- The bundle installs with a dry run.
- Media kit, package/rate, campaign proposal, negotiation, and recap/renewal
  scenarios route to the intended skills.
- Outputs include concrete artifacts: pitch email, media kit section, package
  table, rate-card draft, campaign proposal, rights checklist, negotiation prep,
  deliverables tracker, recap report, or renewal pitch.
- External sends, contract decisions, pricing commitments, and sponsor
  approvals require explicit human review.
