# Creator Business Ops Bundle Brief

## Problem

Creators often grow from solo publishing into a small business with sponsor
pipeline, invoices, budgets, contractors, assistants, assets, rights, and weekly
follow-through. The operational work is scattered across spreadsheets, DMs,
folders, calendars, invoices, and sponsor threads. This bundle creates
reviewable operating artifacts without building a vendor-specific CRM, payment,
project-management, accounting, or storage platform.

## Target User

Influencers, creator-founders, creator managers, assistants, agencies, editors,
and small creator teams coordinating business operations.

## Included Skills

- `creator-invoice-readiness`: prepare sponsor invoice handoff checklists,
  deliverable proof, approval evidence, missing fields, and review owners.
- `creator-production-ops-brief`: produce production budgets, source-asset
  handoffs, assistant/contractor briefs, dependencies, capacity notes, and
  acceptance criteria.
- `owner-dashboard`: create weekly creator ops dashboards.
- `cash-flow-triage`: review receivables, payables, and cash pressure.
- `finance-close-checklist`: prepare month-end creator business checklists.
- `pricing-margin-review`: review campaign, merch, service, and production
  economics.
- `ops-bottleneck-review`: diagnose production, delegation, capacity, and
  workflow constraints.
- `vendor-negotiation-prep`: support editor, agency, tool, contractor, and
  vendor discussions.
- `action-tracker`: track weekly follow-ups, blockers, and ownerless tasks.
- `deliverables-tracker`: track sponsor deliverables, approvals, links, and
  invoice milestones.
- `sales-pipeline-management`: support sponsor CRM stages and follow-up hygiene.
- `dashboard-design`, `metric-definition`, `metric-narrative`, and
  `variance-commentary`: define ops metrics and reporting views.
- `kb-from-resolution` and `tutorial-writing`: create lightweight SOPs,
  assistant instructions, and reusable internal docs.
- `usage-rights-checklist`: support rights-aware asset and sponsor operations.
- `brand-pitch`: support sponsor follow-up copy when pipeline work needs it.
- `concise-technical-writing`: tighten operating briefs and internal docs.

## Context Files

- `CREATOR_OPS_DASHBOARD.md`: weekly priorities, metrics, blockers, decisions,
  and owner actions.
- `SPONSOR_PIPELINE.md`: sponsor stages, follow-ups, deliverables, approvals,
  and invoice milestones.
- `INVOICE_READINESS.md`: invoice fields, deliverable proof, payment terms,
  accounting owner, and missing data.
- `PRODUCTION_OPS.md`: production plan, budget, source assets, owners, due
  dates, dependencies, and review gates.
- `ASSET_RIGHTS.md`: rights, usage terms, source assets, approvals, expiries,
  and restricted files.
- `CONTRACTOR_HANDOFFS.md`: contractor/assistant roles, briefs, specs,
  acceptance criteria, and open questions.

## MCP Preset Intent

Use documentation research for current platform, accounting, payment, CRM,
storage, payroll, vendor, and rights guidance when exact tactical details
matter. Keep accounting, banking, payroll, CRM, payment, project-management,
social, storage, publishing, and contractor systems read-only unless the user
explicitly approves a specific action.

## Safety Rules

- Do not send invoices, emails, sponsor updates, payment reminders, contractor
  messages, or approval notices without explicit approval.
- Do not change accounting, banking, payroll, CRM, payment,
  project-management, social, storage, publishing, or contractor systems without
  approval.
- Do not invent balances, invoice status, payment terms, sponsor approvals,
  contractor facts, rights ownership, usage permissions, tax details, revenue,
  or task completion.
- Treat tax, accounting, employment, legal, contract, payroll, rights, and
  payment disputes as review-required.
- Separate confirmed facts, assumptions, missing data, risks, and suggested next
  actions.
- Avoid platform-specific implementation unless current official docs are
  checked and the user asks for that vendor.

## Pilot Metrics

- Operational: time from scattered ops context to weekly review or handoff
  artifact.
- Quality: creator/manager edit rate on invoice, production, contractor, and
  dashboard artifacts.
- Adoption: percent of operating artifacts used in weekly review.
- Economic: reduced admin time, fewer missed follow-ups, and clearer cash or
  invoice readiness.

## Acceptance Criteria

- The bundle installs with a dry run.
- Weekly dashboard, sponsor pipeline, invoice readiness, production budget,
  assistant/contractor handoff, and asset checklist scenarios route to intended
  skills.
- Outputs include concrete artifacts: weekly ops dashboard, sponsor pipeline
  review, invoice readiness checklist, production ops brief, contractor handoff,
  asset checklist, SOP, or action tracker.
- External sends, system writes, payment actions, employment/contractor
  decisions, rights conclusions, and finance/accounting actions require human
  review.
