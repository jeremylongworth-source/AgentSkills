# Creator Community Bundle Brief

## Problem

Creators often receive comments, DMs, member posts, poll responses, fan
feedback, moderation issues, and community prompts across many channels. The
work is emotionally noisy, time-sensitive, and easy to over-automate. This
bundle creates reviewable community artifacts without building a social inbox,
moderation bot, or platform-specific community manager.

## Target User

Influencers, creator-founders, community managers, creator teams, agencies,
assistants, editors, and managers handling audience relationships.

## Included Skills

- `community-triage`: classify comments, DMs, replies, member posts, and fan
  feedback by intent, urgency, sentiment, risk, owner, and next action.
- `community-moderation-brief`: prepare moderation and escalation briefs with
  evidence, options, rationale, missing context, and review owners.
- `reply-drafting`: draft reviewed public or private community replies after
  triage.
- `ticket-triage` and `escalation-pack`: handle support-like, account, sponsor,
  safety, or policy escalation packets.
- `feedback-synthesis`: summarize audience themes and product/content signals.
- `customer-research-validation` and `experiment-design-validation`: design
  polls, audience learning plans, and lightweight community tests.
- `content-pillars`, `caption-writer`, `content-calendar`, and
  `publishing-checklist`: plan community prompts, event touchpoints, and
  engagement cadence.
- `metric-definition`, `metric-narrative`, and
  `creator-content-performance-review`: define and summarize engagement signals.
- `concise-technical-writing`: tighten community briefs, summaries, and
  reviewer notes.

## Context Files

- `COMMUNITY_POLICY.md`: rules, escalation boundaries, moderation principles,
  and voice guidance.
- `COMMENT_DM_QUEUE.md`: comments, DMs, member posts, source links, status,
  owner, urgency, and next action.
- `RESPONSE_LIBRARY.md`: approved voice patterns, reusable replies, and topics
  requiring review.
- `AUDIENCE_FEEDBACK.md`: recurring audience requests, objections, questions,
  content ideas, and product/offer signals.
- `COMMUNITY_EVENTS.md`: prompts, live sessions, member events, polls, and
  post-event follow-up.
- `MODERATION_LOG.md`: review decisions, rationale, escalations, and unresolved
  policy questions.

## MCP Preset Intent

Use documentation research only when current platform, disclosure, safety,
moderation, privacy, or community-tool rules matter. Keep social, community,
support, CRM, publishing, moderation, and member systems read-only unless the
user explicitly approves a specific action.

## Safety Rules

- Do not send replies, delete comments, ban or mute users, approve members,
  change roles, publish posts, report accounts, or update community rules
  without explicit approval.
- Do not invent audience quotes, identities, sentiment, member status, threats,
  policy violations, private facts, prior warnings, screenshots, or engagement
  metrics.
- Escalate harassment, threats, self-harm, minors, doxxing, hate, privacy
  issues, payment/account issues, legal claims, sponsor conflicts, and crisis or
  reputation risks.
- Separate confirmed facts, assumptions, missing context, proposed public reply,
  internal notes, moderation rationale, and approval owner.
- Avoid platform-specific enforcement guidance unless current official docs are
  checked and the user asks for that vendor.

## Pilot Metrics

- Operational: time from raw comments/DMs to triage queue or engagement plan.
- Quality: creator or community-manager edit rate on reply and moderation
  drafts.
- Adoption: percent of triage or engagement artifacts used in weekly community
  review.
- Trust: reduction in unowned escalations, missed high-risk items, or unclear
  reply ownership.

## Acceptance Criteria

- The bundle installs with a dry run.
- Comment/DM triage, feedback synthesis, poll planning, moderation escalation,
  community engagement, and event planning scenarios route to intended skills.
- Outputs include concrete artifacts: triage table, moderation brief, reply
  queue, feedback synthesis, poll plan, engagement calendar, event prompt plan,
  or escalation packet.
- External sends, moderation actions, platform changes, support/account actions,
  policy decisions, public crisis statements, and sponsor-facing statements
  require human review.
