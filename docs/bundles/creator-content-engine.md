# Creator Content Engine Bundle Brief

## Problem

Creators need a repeatable system for turning ideas into publishable content
across short-form video, long-form video, newsletters, podcasts, social posts,
and campaign calendars. Generic content advice is not enough; the workflow must
produce scripts, hooks, captions, calendars, repurposing plans, and publishing
checks that preserve trust.

## Target User

Influencers, social creators, creator-founders, content strategists, editors,
creator assistants, and small creator teams.

## Included Skills

- `content-pillars`: define audience promise, recurring themes, and content
  lanes.
- `short-form-hooks`: write truthful hooks and first beats for short-form
  content.
- `video-script`: draft video scripts, visual notes, CTAs, and cutdown ideas.
- `ai-video-prompt-pack`: create AI video prompt packs with timing, variants,
  negative prompts, safe areas, and platform/model constraints.
- `thumbnail-title-brief`: create title packs and thumbnail creative briefs.
- `social-asset-production`: create profile images, banners, post images,
  article covers, thumbnails, and crop-safe export checks.
- `caption-writer`: write social captions, CTAs, community prompts, and
  disclosure notes.
- `content-calendar`: build 7-day, 30-day, weekly, and campaign calendars.
- `longform-to-shorts`: repurpose long-form assets into short-form clips.
- `newsletter-repurpose`: adapt creator content into newsletters or social
  cutdowns.
- `podcast-outline`: create episode outlines, questions, and clip moments.
- `owned-social-profile-audit`: audit bios, profile assets, links, pinned or
  featured posts, visibility risks, and conversion paths.
- `brand-social-launch-pack`: coordinate profile updates, launch posts, pinned
  content, social assets, replies, and publishing checks.
- `publishing-checklist`: create pre-publish QA and post-publish monitoring
  checklists.
- `content-seo-strategy`: support search-oriented content planning.
- `concise-technical-writing`: tighten scripts, briefs, captions, and calendars.

## Context Files

- `CREATOR_VOICE.md`: voice, tone, boundaries, claims, and examples.
- `CONTENT_PILLARS.md`: current pillars, series, proof points, and avoid list.
- `PLATFORM_NOTES.md`: supported platforms, format preferences, and current
  constraints.
- `SPONSOR_DISCLOSURE.md`: disclosure, affiliate, gift, and material-connection
  rules.
- `CONTENT_ASSET_LIBRARY.md`: reusable assets, rights, clips, thumbnails, and
  source material.

## MCP Preset Intent

Use documentation research for current platform specs, disclosure guidance, and
publisher requirements when exact tactical details matter. Keep publishing,
scheduling, social account, and analytics tools read-only unless the user
explicitly approves an action.

## Safety Rules

- Do not promise virality, reach, revenue, watch time, sponsor acceptance, or
  algorithmic outcomes.
- Do not invent creator credentials, audience metrics, personal stories,
  testimonials, sponsor claims, or product results.
- Do not publish, schedule, send, or approve content automatically.
- Require review for sponsored, affiliate, AI-generated, regulated, crisis,
  legal, financial, health, or reputationally sensitive content.
- Verify current platform requirements, disclosure rules, format specs, and
  monetization rules when tactical details matter.

## Pilot Metrics

- Operational: time from idea list to publish-ready content calendar.
- Quality: creator edit rate on hooks, scripts, captions, and calendar entries.
- Adoption: percent of generated content assets retained or scheduled after
  human review.
- Economic: reduced production planning time and increased sponsor/content
  reporting readiness.

## Acceptance Criteria

- The bundle installs with a dry run.
- Content pillar, short-form script, AI video prompt, social asset, profile
  audit, launch pack, calendar, repurposing, and publishing checklist scenarios
  route to the intended skills.
- Outputs include concrete artifacts: pillar map, hook pack, video script,
  title/thumbnail brief, caption bank, content calendar, repurposing plan,
  newsletter draft, podcast outline, or publishing checklist.
- Content remains draft-first and requires explicit approval before publishing.
