# Creator Monetization Bundle Brief

## Problem

Creators often have attention but no clear owned-revenue system. Sponsorships
alone do not cover digital products, courses, memberships, paid communities,
merch, affiliates, pricing, cash impact, launch planning, or retention. This
bundle helps map monetization options into reviewable artifacts without
building vendor-specific checkout, course, ecommerce, or membership systems.

## Target User

Influencers, creator-founders, solo creators, creator managers, assistants,
agencies, and small creator teams planning owned revenue streams.

## Included Skills

- `creator-offer-ladder`: map free, affiliate, low-ticket, recurring, sponsor,
  community, merch, service, and premium offers into a trust-aware ladder.
- `creator-owned-product-launch-plan`: plan launches for digital products,
  courses, merch, memberships, paid communities, bundles, waitlists, and offer
  pages.
- `pricing-packaging-strategy`: design pricing models, tiers, value metrics,
  validation plans, and billing implications.
- `pricing-margin-review`: review margin, discounting, capacity, and package
  economics.
- `affiliate-performance-report`: review affiliate revenue, commissions,
  refunds, and attribution caveats from supplied data.
- `marketing-analytics-attribution`: support UTMs, links, affiliate tracking,
  paid amplification, and attribution caveats.
- `metric-definition`, `metric-narrative`, `dashboard-design`, `funnel-analysis`,
  and `retention-analysis`: support monetization KPIs, dashboards, funnels, and
  retention.
- `content-calendar`, `caption-writer`, `publishing-checklist`, and
  `content-seo-strategy`: support launch content and disclosure-safe publishing.
- `owner-dashboard` and `cash-flow-triage`: support business and cash review.
- `rate-card-builder`, `sponsorship-package`, `campaign-recap`, and
  `renewal-pitch`: support sponsor-adjacent monetization.
- `experiment-design-validation` and `experiment-readout`: support demand tests
  and post-launch readouts.
- `marketing-positioning-strategy` and `concise-technical-writing`: support
  offer positioning and clear copy.

## Context Files

- `CREATOR_OFFERS.md`: current offers, prices, audience segments, proof, and
  trust boundaries.
- `MONETIZATION_METRICS.md`: revenue, conversion, retention, affiliate,
  dashboard, and source definitions.
- `LAUNCH_PLAN.md`: launch dates, assets, owners, channels, review gates, and
  pause criteria.
- `PRICING_ASSUMPTIONS.md`: price points, margin inputs, discounts, costs,
  refunds, platform fees, and unknowns.
- `CHECKOUT_REVIEW.md`: payment, refund, tax, legal, platform, subscription, and
  consumer-protection review items.

## MCP Preset Intent

Use documentation research for current platform, payment, marketplace,
affiliate, subscription, email, tax, refund, and monetization rules when exact
tactical details matter. Keep ecommerce, payment, email, affiliate, course,
membership, social, and analytics tools read-only unless the user explicitly
approves a specific action.

## Safety Rules

- Do not connect directly to Shopify, Stripe, Patreon, Kajabi, Gumroad,
  Substack, YouTube, TikTok, Meta, Amazon, affiliate networks, email tools, or
  payment systems unless the user explicitly approves a specific action.
- Do not publish offers, change pricing, issue discounts, update checkout
  pages, send emails, launch campaigns, create coupons, or modify payment
  settings automatically.
- Do not promise revenue, conversion rates, subscriber growth, affiliate income,
  sponsor acceptance, sales, ROI, platform approval, or launch outcomes.
- Do not invent audience metrics, revenue, conversion data, costs, margins,
  testimonials, customer demand, refund rates, or affiliate terms.
- Treat tax, legal, accounting, consumer protection, platform policy, payment
  processing, refunds, subscriptions, and contractor/vendor terms as
  review-required areas.
- Require explicit human review before public pricing, checkout changes, paid
  community rules, refund policies, affiliate disclosures, sponsor claims, or
  financial reporting.

## Pilot Metrics

- Operational: time from monetization idea to reviewed offer ladder or launch
  plan.
- Quality: creator edit rate on offer, pricing, launch, and review-gate
  artifacts.
- Adoption: percent of generated monetization artifacts approved for testing.
- Economic: reduced planning time and better visibility into cash, margin, and
  launch risks.

## Acceptance Criteria

- The bundle installs with a dry run.
- Offer ladder, owned-product launch, membership/community pricing, merch
  margin, affiliate review, and monetization dashboard scenarios route to the
  intended skills.
- Outputs include concrete artifacts: offer ladder, owned-product launch plan,
  pricing package, membership model, merch margin review, affiliate report,
  dashboard spec, or validation plan.
- Public pricing, checkout changes, payment settings, affiliate disclosures,
  sponsor claims, and financial reporting require explicit human review.
