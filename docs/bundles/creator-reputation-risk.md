# Creator Reputation Risk Bundle Brief

## Problem

Creators face trust and reputation risk across sponsored posts, affiliate
content, AI-assisted production, third-party assets, platform policy, community
backlash, brand-safety concerns, and public response decisions. The risk is
important, but it should not become an automated legal, crisis, moderation, or
platform-monitoring system. This bundle produces review-only risk memos and
hold/revise/go recommendations.

## Target User

Influencers, creator-founders, creator managers, community managers, agencies,
brand-partnership leads, assistants, editors, and creator teams.

## Included Skills

- `creator-reputation-risk-review`: prepare reputation risk memos with
  disclosure, rights, AI-labeling, platform-policy, brand-safety, escalation,
  and approval checks.
- `publishing-checklist`: review pre-publish disclosure, rights, AI, copyright,
  platform-policy, and post-publish monitoring needs.
- `caption-writer`: draft disclosure-safe public copy and safer caption
  variants.
- `usage-rights-checklist`: identify usage, licensing, whitelisting,
  exclusivity, and third-party asset review questions.
- `community-triage` and `community-moderation-brief`: triage audience reaction
  and prepare moderation-sensitive review packets.
- `reply-drafting` and `escalation-pack`: prepare reviewed response drafts and
  internal legal, safety, sponsor, privacy, or executive handoffs.
- `risk-register`: maintain broader reputation and customer-trust risk
  registers.
- `ai-risk-register` and `ai-governance-checklist`: review synthetic media,
  AI-assisted production, likeness, voice, disclosure, data, and human-review
  controls.
- `campaign-proposal` and `sponsorship-package`: review sponsor-facing campaign
  framing, deliverables, exclusions, and brand-safety notes.
- `concise-technical-writing`: tighten reviewer memos and approval notes.

## Context Files

- `REPUTATION_REVIEW.md`: issue summary, supplied evidence, assumptions, risk
  level, recommendation, reviewer, and approval state.
- `DISCLOSURE_AND_RIGHTS.md`: sponsor, affiliate, gifted, AI, copyright,
  licensing, usage, and asset-source notes.
- `BRAND_SAFETY.md`: sponsor sensitivities, excluded claims, restricted topics,
  brand voice, and escalation thresholds.
- `PLATFORM_POLICY_NOTES.md`: current-source links and unresolved vendor-policy
  questions.
- `PUBLIC_RESPONSE_DRAFTS.md`: approved response options, apology/correction
  drafts, internal notes, and required review gates.
- `ESCALATION_LOG.md`: legal, safety, privacy, sponsor, platform, or manager
  handoffs.

## MCP Preset Intent

Use documentation research only when current official platform, disclosure,
paid-content, AI-labeling, or content-policy rules materially affect the answer.
Keep social, publishing, moderation, community, support, CRM, sponsor, platform,
and legal systems read-only unless the user explicitly approves a specific
action.

## Safety Rules

- This is workflow support only, not legal, compliance, crisis, copyright,
  privacy, platform-enforcement, sponsorship, or trust-and-safety advice.
- Do not publish, delete, reply, apologize publicly, report accounts, ban or
  mute users, edit settings, update rules, contact sponsors, or contact
  platforms without explicit approval.
- Do not claim legal compliance, copyright clearance, fair use, disclosure
  sufficiency, platform-policy compliance, or brand safety certification.
- Do not invent audience reaction, creator intent, platform rules, sponsor
  positions, screenshots, consent, licenses, permissions, threats, or legal
  exposure.
- Verify current official platform or disclosure guidance before tactical
  platform-specific recommendations.
- Escalate legal claims, minors, harassment, doxxing, threats, self-harm,
  privacy issues, sponsor conflicts, paid claims, AI likeness/voice concerns,
  and public backlash.

## Pilot Metrics

- Operational: time from risky content or issue packet to review memo.
- Quality: creator, manager, counsel, or sponsor edit rate on risk memos and
  public-response drafts.
- Adoption: percent of risky content decisions that use a hold/revise/go memo.
- Trust: reduction in missed disclosure, rights, AI-labeling, or escalation
  checks.

## Acceptance Criteria

- The bundle installs with a dry run.
- Disclosure, AI-labeling, rights/copyright, brand-safety, controversy, and
  platform-policy scenarios route to intended skills.
- Outputs include concrete artifacts: reputation risk memo, disclosure
  checklist, rights risk list, AI-labeling review, brand-safety memo,
  controversy response brief, escalation packet, or hold/revise/go
  recommendation.
- Legal, compliance, copyright, platform-policy, crisis, moderation, sponsor,
  and public-response decisions remain human-reviewed.
