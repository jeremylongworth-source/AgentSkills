# Operating Cadence Bundle Brief

## Problem

Leadership teams need agents to turn meetings, OKRs, metrics, decisions, risks,
dependencies, and follow-up into a repeatable operating rhythm instead of
one-off summaries.

## Target User

COOs, chiefs of staff, CEOs, founders, operators, and leadership teams running
weekly, monthly, and quarterly operating cadences.

## Included Skills

- `executive-briefing`: prepare leadership agendas, MBRs, operating updates, and
  planning briefs.
- `decision-memo`: maintain decision registers and frame tradeoffs.
- `risk-register`: track escalations, risks, dependencies, and mitigations.
- `action-tracker`: capture owners, deadlines, blockers, and follow-up.
- `metric-narrative` and `variance-commentary`: explain scorecard, OKR, and KPI
  movement.
- `product-brainstorming-planning`: clarify ambiguous planning choices.
- `concise-technical-writing`: tighten cadence artifacts.

## Context Files

- `OPERATING_CADENCE.md`: meeting rhythm, standard agendas, decision rules, and
  follow-up expectations.
- `OKR_SCORECARD.md`: objectives, key results, owners, status, and confidence.
- `DECISION_REGISTER.md`: decisions, owners, rationale, and review dates.
- `ESCALATION_TRACKER.md`: escalations, blockers, dependencies, owners, and due
  dates.
- `MBR_TEMPLATE.md`: monthly business review sections, metrics, risks, and
  required pre-reads.

## MCP Preset Intent

Use documentation research for public context when needed. Keep project,
finance, customer, HR, and internal operating systems read-only unless a
reviewer approves a write action.

## Safety Rules

- Draft-first and review-first by default.
- Do not invent metrics, OKR status, owners, deadlines, approvals, or decisions.
- Separate meeting notes, decisions, risks, and actions.
- Require leadership review before official OKR changes, staffing commitments,
  customer commitments, budget decisions, or public reporting.

## Pilot Metrics

- Operational: time to prepare weekly leadership agenda or MBR.
- Quality: missed-action rate and owner correction rate.
- Adoption: percentage of action items accepted into cadence.
- Economic: meeting prep time saved or decision follow-through improvement.

## Acceptance Criteria

- The bundle installs with a dry run.
- Weekly leadership, OKR review, and MBR scenarios route to the intended skills.
- Outputs are cadence artifacts, not generic leadership advice.
- Decisions, actions, risks, and metrics are separated.
