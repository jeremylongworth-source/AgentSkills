# Owner Operator OS Bundle Brief

## Problem

Small business owners and operators need practical AI workflows for cash,
pricing, vendors, operations, and monthly decision-making without enterprise
complexity or unreviewed financial/legal commitments.

## Target User

Small business owners, solo founders, agency owners, local business operators,
franchise operators, and practical owner-operators.

## Included Skills

- `owner-dashboard`: create owner dashboards and business snapshots.
- `cash-flow-triage`: summarize cash pressure, missing data, and action options.
- `pricing-margin-review`: review pricing, margin pressure, and packaging
  options.
- `vendor-negotiation-prep`: prepare vendor renewal and negotiation briefs.
- `ops-bottleneck-review`: map operational bottlenecks and practical fixes.
- `action-tracker`: turn owner decisions, cash actions, vendor follow-ups,
  pricing changes, and operations fixes into owners, deadlines, blockers, and
  follow-through.
- `metric-narrative` and `variance-commentary`: explain supplied metrics and
  changes.
- `pricing-packaging-strategy`: support pricing and packaging context.
- `concise-technical-writing`: tighten owner artifacts.

## Context Files

- `OWNER_PRIORITIES.md`: current owner goals, constraints, and decision horizon.
- `BUSINESS_SNAPSHOT.md`: cash, revenue, customers, staff, vendors, and risks.
- `CASH_FLOW_CONTEXT.md`: receivables, payables, payroll, debt, taxes, and
  obligations.
- `PRICING_MARGIN_CONTEXT.md`: services, prices, costs, margin assumptions, and
  customer segments.
- `VENDOR_REGISTER.md`: vendors, spend, renewal dates, dependencies, and review
  owners.

## MCP Preset Intent

Use documentation research for public market, vendor, and pricing context. Keep
banking, accounting, payroll, tax, CRM, vendor, and customer systems read-only
unless the owner explicitly approves a write action.

## Safety Rules

- Business analysis support only; not legal, tax, accounting, lending,
  investment, employment, or financial advice.
- Do not invent cash balances, revenue, margins, payroll, tax obligations,
  contract terms, customer facts, or vendor commitments.
- Separate confirmed data, assumptions, missing data, and action options.
- Require owner or professional review before finance, legal, tax, employment,
  vendor, debt, customer, or public pricing commitments.

## Pilot Metrics

- Operational: time to monthly owner dashboard or cash triage summary.
- Quality: reviewer correction rate and missing-data count.
- Adoption: owner actions accepted for follow-up.
- Economic: owner decision-prep time saved.

## Acceptance Criteria

- The bundle installs with a dry run.
- Dashboard, cash-flow, pricing, vendor, and operations scenarios route to the
  intended skills.
- Outputs are practical owner artifacts, not generic business advice.
- High-risk financial, legal, tax, employment, customer, and vendor actions are
  marked for review.
