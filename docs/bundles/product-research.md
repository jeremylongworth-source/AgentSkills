# Product Research Bundle Brief

## Problem

Product teams need agents to convert feedback and evidence into clear product
decisions, PRDs, validation plans, and analytics needs without overstating weak
signals.

## Target User

Product managers, founders, researchers, designers, growth/product analysts, and
engineering leads shaping product work.

## Included Skills

- `guided-requirements-interview`: ask focused questions when product context is
  incomplete before drafting a PRD or product brief.
- `feedback-synthesis`: cluster feedback into themes, evidence, confidence, and
  opportunities.
- `write-spec`: turn evidence and decisions into PRDs, feature specs, and
  acceptance criteria.
- `product-brainstorming-planning`: shape broad ideas before implementation.
- `customer-research-validation`: plan and synthesize customer evidence.
- `competitive-market-research`: verify market and competitor context.
- `experiment-design-validation`: define hypotheses, metrics, and decision
  rules.
- `product-analytics-instrumentation`: define telemetry and tracking plans.
- `concise-technical-writing`: tighten specs and stakeholder summaries.

## Context Files

- `FEEDBACK_SOURCES.md`: source inventory, segments, date ranges, and known
  biases.
- `PRODUCT_DECISION_RULES.md`: decision owners, thresholds, and escalation rules.
- `ROADMAP_CONTEXT.md`: current roadmap themes, constraints, and non-goals.
- `RESEARCH_REPOSITORY.md`: where customer evidence, interviews, and findings
  live.

## MCP Preset Intent

Use documentation research for current public docs, API behavior, and market
research. Keep product analytics, customer research, and roadmap systems
read-only unless a reviewer approves a write action.

## Safety Rules

- Keep evidence, assumptions, and recommendations separate.
- Do not invent counts, customer quotes, market facts, or analytics figures.
- Do not make roadmap, pricing, legal, support, or customer commitments.
- Escalate when evidence is thin, biased, sensitive, or tied to high-risk
  customer or business decisions.

## Pilot Metrics

- Operational: time from feedback corpus to draft spec.
- Quality: stakeholder revision rounds and reviewer correction rate.
- Adoption: PM acceptance rate.
- Economic: backlog grooming time saved.

## Acceptance Criteria

- The bundle installs with a dry run.
- Feedback-to-PRD scenarios route to synthesis, spec writing, research,
  experiment, analytics, and writing skills.
- Outputs separate raw evidence from interpretation and recommendation.
- PRDs include goals, non-goals, acceptance criteria, validation, and risks.
