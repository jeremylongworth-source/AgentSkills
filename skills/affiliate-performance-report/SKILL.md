# Affiliate Performance Report

## Core Workflow

1. Identify affiliate program, links or codes, source data, date range,
   commission model, content assets, traffic sources, attribution rules, and
   reporting question.
2. Summarize clicks, conversions, conversion rate, revenue or commission, AOV,
   EPC, refund/cancel caveats, and top content when supplied.
3. Compare performance by content, platform, product, link, code, audience
   segment, and campaign when data allows.
4. Separate tracked performance from untracked influence and attribution limits.
5. Add disclosure, tracking, affiliate-window, refund, and data-quality caveats.
6. Recommend content, offer, link placement, disclosure, or tracking tests.

## Safety Rules

- Do not invent clicks, revenue, commission, conversion rates, refunds,
  attribution, or sponsor outcomes.
- Do not provide tax, accounting, investment, or financial advice.
- Do not hide affiliate relationships or material connections.
- Require review before sponsor, finance, public, or renewal use.

## Deliverable Shape

For affiliate performance reports, provide:

- Source and date range
- Performance table
- Top products, links, codes, or content
- Attribution and cookie-window caveats
- Disclosure checks
- Test recommendations
- Review notes
