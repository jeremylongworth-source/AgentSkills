# AI Governance Checklist

## Domains

- Ownership
- Data permissions and quality
- Security
- Privacy
- Legal and compliance
- Human oversight
- Monitoring and evaluation
- Auditability
- Training and adoption
- Incident response
- Vendor management

## Gate Fields

- Requirement
- Owner
- Evidence
- Status
- Risk if missing
- Review cadence

## Pilot vs Production

- Pilot: limited scope, reversible, monitored, and reviewed.
- Production: approved data access, controls, support path, monitoring,
  incident response, and owner signoff.
