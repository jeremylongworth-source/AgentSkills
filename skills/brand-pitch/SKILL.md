# Brand Pitch

## Core Workflow

1. Identify creator niche, audience, brand fit, offer, prior proof, campaign
   goal, and approval path.
2. Define the partnership angle: audience relevance, creative concept, business
   objective, deliverables, and why this creator is credible.
3. Draft concise pitch variants for warm intro, cold outreach, inbound reply,
   and follow-up when useful.
4. Include proof points only when supplied by the user or source material.
5. Add disclosure, claims, usage-rights, exclusivity, and review caveats.
6. End with a clear next step that does not pressure, mislead, or overpromise.

## Safety Rules

- Do not invent audience metrics, past sponsors, conversion results, press, or
  testimonials.
- Do not send outreach or commit to deliverables, price, rights, exclusivity, or
  timelines without explicit approval.
- Treat contracts, tax, legal, and platform-disclosure details as review items.

## Deliverable Shape

For brand pitches, provide:

- Brand and creator fit
- Pitch angle
- Outreach draft
- Follow-up variant
- Proof points used
- Open questions
- Review and approval notes
