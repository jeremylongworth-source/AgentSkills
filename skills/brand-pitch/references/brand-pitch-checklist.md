# Brand Pitch Checklist

## Inputs

- Creator niche and voice
- Audience segment
- Brand category and fit
- Campaign goal
- Prior proof supplied by the user
- Desired next step

## Pitch Elements

- Relevance hook
- Creator credibility
- Audience fit
- Creative concept
- Suggested deliverables
- Proof points
- Next step

## Review

- Metrics are sourced
- Claims are supportable
- Disclosure needs are visible
- Rights and exclusivity are not assumed
- No send without approval
