# Brand Social Launch Pack Checklist

## Inputs

- Launch object: brand, repo, article, product, service, feature, campaign.
- Audience: buyers, developers, creators, operators, investors, community.
- Primary CTA: intake, signup, repo star, article read, waitlist, reply, demo.
- Proof: screenshot, repo, case study, article, demo, benchmark, public asset.
- Channels: X, Facebook, LinkedIn, YouTube, Instagram, TikTok, newsletter.
- Owner actions: what the account owner must manually publish or verify.

## Message Hierarchy

1. What is launching?
2. Who is it for?
3. What problem does it solve?
4. What proof or artifact backs the claim?
5. What should the viewer do next?
6. What should not distract from the main CTA?

## Launch Assets

- Primary post copy
- Image or video prompt brief
- Article or long-form explanation
- Follow-up reply or comment
- Profile link recommendation
- Pinned or featured recommendation
- Cross-post variants
- Alt text or accessibility note

## Verification Gates

- All links resolve to the intended destination.
- Public/logged-out view shows the expected profile and post state where possible.
- Pinned or featured content supports the main CTA.
- Asset crop and text legibility have been checked.
- Platform-specific constraints have been verified from official docs when exact
  tactical guidance matters.
- Human owner confirms publishing, pinning, and audience controls.
