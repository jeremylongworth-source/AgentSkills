# Campaign Proposal

## Core Workflow

1. Identify sponsor objective, creator audience, campaign theme, platforms,
   deliverables, timeline, approvals, metrics, and constraints.
2. Build a proposal with strategic fit, creative concept, content plan,
   deliverables, timeline, measurement, assumptions, and next steps.
3. Include disclosure, usage-rights, exclusivity, revision, and approval notes.
4. Separate confirmed facts from assumptions and placeholders.
5. Avoid guaranteed performance claims; define measurement as reporting, not
   promised outcomes.
6. Provide a clear decision path for sponsor review.

## Safety Rules

- Do not promise sales, conversions, views, reach, virality, or ROI.
- Do not invent sponsor goals, creator metrics, audience demographics, or case
  studies.
- Require review before sending externally or accepting terms.

## Deliverable Shape

For campaign proposals, provide:

- Proposal summary
- Sponsor objective
- Creator/audience fit
- Creative concept
- Deliverable plan
- Timeline and approvals
- Measurement plan
- Assumptions and open questions
