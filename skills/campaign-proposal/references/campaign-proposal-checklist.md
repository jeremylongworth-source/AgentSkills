# Campaign Proposal Checklist

## Proposal Sections

- Sponsor objective
- Audience fit
- Campaign concept
- Content angles
- Deliverables
- Timeline
- Approval flow
- Measurement plan
- Assumptions

## Measurement

- Impressions or views when available
- Engagement
- Clicks or affiliate conversions when tracked
- Saves or shares
- Sponsor recap deliverable

## Review

- No guaranteed results
- Disclosure included
- Rights and exclusivity flagged
- Sender approval required
