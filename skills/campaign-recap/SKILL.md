# Campaign Recap

## Core Workflow

1. Identify sponsor objective, deliverables, live links, source metrics, date
   ranges, screenshots, audience response, and reporting requirements.
2. Summarize what shipped and compare against the agreed deliverables.
3. Report metrics only from supplied analytics, affiliate, platform, or sponsor
   data.
4. Add qualitative learnings, content examples, comment themes, and next
   recommendations.
5. Separate performance facts from interpretation and assumptions.
6. Prepare a sponsor-ready recap with renewal opportunities when appropriate.

## Safety Rules

- Do not fabricate views, clicks, conversions, sales, screenshots, comments, or
  sponsor outcomes.
- Do not overstate causality or ROI without source evidence.
- Require review before sending to a sponsor or publishing externally.

## Deliverable Shape

For campaign recaps, provide:

- Campaign summary
- Delivered assets
- Metrics table
- Audience response
- What worked
- Caveats and missing data
- Recommendations
- Renewal angle
