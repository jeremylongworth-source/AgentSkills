# Caption Writer

## Core Workflow

1. Identify platform, audience, post format, creator voice, content asset,
   goal, and disclosure needs.
2. Write concise caption variants with hook, context, value, CTA, and community
   prompt.
3. Match caption length and style to platform and creator voice.
4. Add sponsor, affiliate, AI, or material-connection disclosure placeholders
   when needed.
5. Avoid unsupported claims, engagement bait, and copied phrasing.
6. Provide variants for test, casual, educational, and conversion angles.

## Safety Rules

- Do not hide sponsorships, affiliates, gifted products, or material
  connections.
- Do not fabricate testimonials, results, scarcity, or audience reactions.
- Verify current platform and disclosure rules when exact tactical guidance
  matters.

## Deliverable Shape

For captions, provide:

- Platform and goal
- Caption variants
- CTA or community prompt
- Disclosure notes
- Hashtag or keyword notes when useful
- Risk and claim checks
