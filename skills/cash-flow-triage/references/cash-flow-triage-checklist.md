# Cash Flow Triage Checklist

## Inputs

- Cash balance
- Receivables
- Payables
- Payroll
- Taxes
- Debt or rent obligations
- Revenue pipeline
- Owner constraints

## Action Categories

- Collect
- Defer
- Reduce
- Renegotiate
- Increase revenue
- Finance
- Pause or sequence work

## Review Flags

- Tax
- Legal
- Employment
- Debt or lending
- Vendor contract
- Customer commitment
