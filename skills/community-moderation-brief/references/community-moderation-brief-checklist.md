# Community Moderation Brief Checklist

## Required Context

- Item or thread summary
- Channel and public/private status
- Community rule, sponsor rule, or brand guideline if available
- Prior actions or warnings if confirmed
- Audience impact
- Deadline or escalation pressure
- Decision owner

## Option Types

- No action
- Reply publicly
- Reply privately
- Hold for review
- Hide or remove recommendation
- Escalate to owner, support, legal, safety, sponsor, or platform reviewer
- Convert pattern into rule, FAQ, or community guidance

## Review Gates

- Legal or compliance concern
- Threat, self-harm, hate, harassment, doxxing, or minors
- Payment, refund, account, or customer support issue
- Copyright, platform policy, sponsorship disclosure, or privacy issue
- Sponsor, employer, partner, press, or public backlash risk

## Output Checks

- Rationale is tied to supplied evidence.
- The recommendation is clearly a draft for review.
- Public reply drafts and internal notes are separated.
- No moderation or platform action is performed without approval.
