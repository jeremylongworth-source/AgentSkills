# Community Triage Checklist

## Inputs

- Channel or source
- Public/private status
- Message text or summary
- Thread context
- Audience or member segment if confirmed
- Brand voice or community policy
- Known sponsor, customer, support, or safety relevance

## Intent Types

- Appreciation
- Question
- Support request
- Complaint
- Content suggestion
- Product or offer feedback
- Sponsor or partnership signal
- Community conflict
- Moderation concern
- Safety concern
- Spam or abuse

## Risk Flags

- Threat, self-harm, harassment, hate, doxxing, or minors
- Legal, privacy, copyright, sponsorship, or platform policy issue
- Payment, refund, account, or customer support issue
- Potential public backlash or reputation risk
- Sponsor, employer, partner, or press visibility

## Output Checks

- Confirmed facts are separated from assumptions.
- Public reply candidates are distinct from internal notes.
- Escalation items name the reason and owner.
- No send, delete, ban, role, publish, or tool-change action is implied without
  approval.
