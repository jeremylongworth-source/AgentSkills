# Competitive Research Checklist

- Strategic question is clear.
- Direct, indirect, and status-quo alternatives are included.
- Sources are current and cited when external research is used.
- Claims distinguish fact, inference, and opinion.
- Comparison includes buyer, use case, price/packaging, proof, features, integrations, channels, and support.
- Review mining captures repeated praise, complaints, switching triggers, and unmet needs.
- Output explains what the research changes about positioning, product, sales, or marketing.
