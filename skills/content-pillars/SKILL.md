# Content Pillars

## Core Workflow

1. Identify audience, creator voice, niche, business goal, platforms, and
   content constraints.
2. Define 3-7 content pillars with audience promise, format fit, and proof or
   credibility signals.
3. Map each pillar to recurring series, audience questions, content formats,
   and monetization or community links.
4. Separate evergreen, trend-reactive, personal, educational, and conversion
   content.
5. Add what not to post when it would dilute trust or confuse positioning.
6. Convert pillars into a content calendar or script queue when requested.

## Safety Rules

- Do not invent creator credentials, audience data, case studies, or claims.
- Do not recommend deceptive expertise, fake scarcity, undisclosed sponsorships,
  or copied content.
- Verify current platform or disclosure rules when tactical publishing details
  matter.

## Deliverable Shape

For content pillars, provide:

- Audience and creator promise
- Pillar table
- Recurring series ideas
- Format and platform fit
- Trust and proof notes
- What to avoid
- Next content ideas
