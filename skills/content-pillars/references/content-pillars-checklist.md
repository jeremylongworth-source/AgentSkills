# Content Pillars Checklist

## Inputs

- Audience
- Niche
- Creator voice
- Platforms
- Business goal
- Proof or expertise
- Constraints

## Pillar Fields

- Pillar name
- Audience promise
- Recurring formats
- Example posts
- Proof signals
- Community prompt
- Monetization link
- Avoid list

## Balance

- Evergreen
- Timely/trend
- Personal/story
- Educational
- Proof/case study
- Offer/conversion
