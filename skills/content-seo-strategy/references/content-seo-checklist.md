# Content SEO Checklist

- Target query or topic has clear user intent.
- Content answers the user's real question before selling.
- Title, description, headings, and URL are descriptive and accurate.
- Page includes original evidence, examples, data, expert input, or first-hand experience where possible.
- Internal links guide users to related education, comparison, conversion, and support pages.
- Images, tables, videos, and tools serve the content rather than decorate it.
- Technical basics are sound: crawlable page, canonical clarity, fast enough load, mobile usability, and no broken links.
- CTA matches funnel stage.
- Measurement includes organic visibility, engagement, assisted conversion, and lead/customer quality.
