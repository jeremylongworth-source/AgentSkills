# Creator Content Performance Review Checklist

## Group By

- Platform
- Format
- Topic or pillar
- Hook type
- Title or thumbnail angle
- Length
- CTA
- Sponsor status
- Publish date

## Metrics

- Views or impressions
- Watch time
- Retention
- Completion rate
- Engagement
- Saves and shares
- Clicks
- Subscriber or follower change
- Conversion when sourced

## Review

- Compare against a clear baseline
- Mark outliers
- Separate findings from hypotheses
- Avoid causation claims without evidence
- Note missing or inconsistent data
