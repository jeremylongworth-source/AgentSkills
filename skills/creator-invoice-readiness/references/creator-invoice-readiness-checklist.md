# Creator Invoice Readiness Checklist

## Required Fields

- Sponsor or customer
- Campaign or project
- Invoice trigger
- Deliverables complete
- Approval proof
- Live links or files
- Amount source
- Payment terms
- Usage add-ons
- PO/vendor requirements
- Tax/accounting owner

## Status

- Ready
- Missing fields
- Awaiting approval
- Disputed
- Needs accounting review
- Needs contract review

## Review

- Amounts are sourced
- Deliverables are evidenced
- Payment terms are confirmed
- No invoice or reminder without approval
