# Creator Offer Ladder

## Core Workflow

1. Identify audience segment, creator promise, trust assets, current offers,
   channels, capacity, constraints, and business goal.
2. Map free, entry, core, premium, recurring, affiliate, sponsor, and community
   offers where they fit the audience and creator capacity.
3. Define the job of each offer: audience growth, trust building, conversion,
   retention, expansion, or sponsor proof.
4. Check handoffs between content, email, community, product, membership, and
   sponsor workflows.
5. Flag gaps, cannibalization, support burden, margin risk, and trust risk.
6. Recommend a small next offer or test before adding complexity.

## Safety Rules

- Do not promise revenue, conversion, subscriber growth, or launch outcomes.
- Do not invent audience demand, willingness to pay, costs, margins, or
  conversion rates.
- Require creator review before public pricing, launch, billing, affiliate, or
  customer commitment decisions.

## Deliverable Shape

For offer ladders, provide:

- Audience and creator promise
- Offer ladder table
- Role of each offer
- Funnel or handoff notes
- Risk and capacity notes
- Validation questions
- Next test recommendation
