# Creator Offer Ladder Checklist

## Offer Types

- Free lead magnet
- Newsletter or community entry point
- Low-ticket digital product
- Core product or course
- Membership or subscription
- Paid community
- Merch or physical product
- Coaching or services
- Affiliate offers
- Sponsorships

## Review

- Audience problem is clear
- Trust path is realistic
- Support burden is visible
- Pricing assumptions are marked
- No revenue promises
