# Creator Owned Product Launch Plan Checklist

## Launch Inputs

- Offer
- Audience
- Buyer promise
- Proof
- Launch goal
- Channels
- Delivery model
- Fulfillment assumptions
- Support needs
- Review owners

## Launch Phases

- Validation
- Waitlist
- Prelaunch
- Launch
- Close or evergreen
- Fulfillment
- Support
- Retrospective

## Review

- Claims are supported
- Scarcity is real
- Pricing approved
- Checkout changes approved
- Refund/support policy reviewed
- Vendor rules verified when tactical
- Metrics are defined
