# Creator Production Ops Brief Checklist

## Brief Sections

- Production goal
- Deliverables
- Owners
- Deadlines
- Source assets
- Budget notes
- Contractor or assistant tasks
- Dependencies
- Publishing needs
- Review gates
- Acceptance criteria

## Risk Checks

- Missing assets
- Rights or usage uncertainty
- Budget approval missing
- Contractor scope unclear
- Capacity bottleneck
- Sponsor dependency
- Platform dependency

## Review

- Confirmed facts separated from assumptions
- No tool updates without approval
- Rights and confidentiality are flagged
- Spend and payment decisions are reviewed
