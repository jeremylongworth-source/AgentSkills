# Creator Reputation Risk Review Checklist

## Required Context

- Content, campaign, reply, or issue summary
- Platform and format
- Sponsor, affiliate, gifted product, or material connection status
- Asset sources and rights status if supplied
- AI-generated or AI-assisted elements if supplied
- Public visibility and audience sensitivity
- Existing community reaction if supplied
- Deadline and approval owner

## Risk Checks

- Sponsor, affiliate, gifted product, or material-connection disclosure
- Usage rights, music, footage, image, logo, likeness, and third-party asset risk
- AI label, synthetic likeness, voice, edit disclosure, and authenticity risk
- Platform policy uncertainty
- Brand safety, audience trust, sensitive claim, or regulated topic risk
- Privacy, minors, harassment, doxxing, threat, or safety risk
- Sponsor, partner, employer, or customer conflict
- Public backlash, apology, correction, or escalation pressure

## Recommendations

- Go after approval
- Revise before review
- Hold for missing facts
- Escalate to legal, manager, sponsor, platform, safety, privacy, or crisis owner
- Do not proceed without qualified review

## Output Checks

- Confirmed facts and assumptions are separated.
- The memo avoids legal, platform, copyright, or disclosure conclusions.
- Public response copy is separated from internal notes.
- No publish, reply, delete, ban, report, sponsor contact, or platform contact
  action is implied without approval.
