# Customer Health Review Checklist

- Customer segment, lifecycle stage, owner, plan, renewal date, and decision
  process are identified when supplied.
- Promised outcomes, current goals, value evidence, and blockers are separated.
- Product usage, adoption milestones, stakeholder engagement, support history,
  open escalations, and billing or contract risks are reviewed.
- Health status distinguishes confirmed facts from assumptions and missing data.
- Churn risk, renewal readiness, expansion/referral potential, and advocacy
  opportunities are tied to evidence.
- Next actions have owner, due date, customer impact, and success criterion.
- QBR or customer-facing language avoids unsupported claims and commitments.
- Repeated patterns are routed to product, support, revenue, or analytics
  follow-up.
