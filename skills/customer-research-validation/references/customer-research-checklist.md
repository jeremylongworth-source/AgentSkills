# Customer Research Checklist

## Planning

- Decision and success criteria are explicit.
- Target participant and exclusion criteria are defined.
- Method matches the question being asked.
- Recruitment source and likely bias are noted.
- Consent, privacy, recording, and data handling are considered.

## Question Quality

- Questions ask about recent real behavior, not only opinions.
- Questions avoid selling the solution.
- Questions separate problem, workflow, alternatives, cost, urgency, and decision process.
- Interview script includes follow-ups for examples, frequency, impact, and workarounds.

## Synthesis

- Raw quotes/evidence are kept separate from conclusions.
- Findings are grouped by theme and segment.
- Confidence level reflects sample size, method, and consistency.
- Recommendations include what to do, what not to do, and what to test next.
