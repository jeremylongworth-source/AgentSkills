# Data Room Checklist

## Folder Areas

- Company overview
- Corporate and legal
- Financials and model
- Metrics and analytics
- Product and roadmap
- Customers and pipeline
- Sales and marketing
- People and hiring
- Security and compliance
- Prior financing
- Investor materials

## Item Fields

- Item
- Status
- Owner
- Sensitivity
- Review owner
- Last updated
- Notes

## Review Checks

- Sensitive documents are marked.
- Legal and finance review needs are visible.
- Missing and stale items have owners.
- Customer and employee data is redacted or excluded unless approved.
