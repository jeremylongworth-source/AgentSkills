# Deal Negotiation Prep Checklist

## Inputs

- Sponsor request
- Creator goals
- Deliverables
- Rate or package
- Timeline
- Usage rights
- Exclusivity
- Reporting

## Negotiation Map

- Must-haves
- Flexible terms
- Counteroffer
- Tradeoffs
- Red flags
- Decision owner

## Review

- No send without approval
- Contract terms need professional review
- Metrics and claims are sourced
- Creator capacity is realistic
