# Decision Memo Checklist

## Decision Frame

- What decision is needed?
- Who owns the decision?
- When is it needed?
- What happens if no decision is made?
- What constraints or commitments already exist?

## Option Criteria

- Strategic fit
- Customer impact
- Revenue or cost impact
- Execution effort
- Risk and reversibility
- Time to learn
- Operational complexity

## Output Checks

- Criteria are explicit before the recommendation.
- Options are real alternatives, not straw choices.
- Unknowns and assumptions are visible.
- Follow-up actions have owners.
- Official or external use is marked for review.
