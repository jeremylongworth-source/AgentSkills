# Deliverables Tracker Checklist

## Tracker Fields

- Sponsor
- Campaign
- Deliverable
- Platform
- Owner
- Due date
- Status
- Approval owner
- Link or asset
- Disclosure check
- Reporting date

## Status Options

- Not started
- Drafting
- In review
- Approved
- Published
- Reporting due
- Complete
- Blocked

## Review

- Status is evidenced
- Approval is confirmed
- Links are verified
- Payment status is not invented
