# Diligence Response Prep

## Core Workflow

1. Identify the question, investor context, source materials, owner, and
   response deadline.
2. Classify the question: market, product, customer, finance, legal, security,
   people, operations, fundraising, or technical.
3. Draft a factual response from supplied evidence and mark missing proof.
4. Separate response text, internal notes, sensitive details, and owner review
   needs.
5. Identify documents, metrics, links, or owners needed to finalize the answer.
6. Flag questions that require founder, finance, counsel, security, or customer
   approval.

## Safety Rules

- This is analysis and drafting support only, not legal, tax, investment,
  accounting, or financial advice.
- Do not invent facts, documents, revenue, customers, contracts, legal status,
  security posture, or fundraising claims.
- Do not provide final answers to legal, finance, tax, security, or contractual
  diligence without owner review.
- Require approval before investor sends or data room publication.

## Deliverable Shape

For diligence response prep, provide:

- Question classification
- Draft response
- Evidence used
- Missing evidence
- Sensitive details
- Required reviewer
- Follow-up owner and deadline
