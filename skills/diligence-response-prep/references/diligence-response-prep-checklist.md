# Diligence Response Prep Checklist

## Question Types

- Market and competition
- Product and roadmap
- Customers and pipeline
- Revenue, finance, and unit economics
- Legal, contracts, and corporate records
- Security, privacy, and compliance
- People and hiring
- Technical architecture
- Prior financing and cap table

## Response Checks

- Response is based on supplied evidence.
- Missing evidence is explicit.
- Sensitive details are separated from sendable text.
- Required reviewer is named.
- Follow-up owner and deadline are clear.

## Escalation

- Finance or accounting question: finance owner review
- Legal, contracts, cap table, or terms: counsel review
- Security or privacy: security/privacy owner review
- Customer-specific details: customer approval or redaction review
