# Escalation Pack Checklist

## Required Fields

- Customer or account identifier approved for internal use
- Ticket link or source
- Product area and environment
- Severity and rationale
- Impacted users, workflow, revenue, or deadline
- Timeline and current status
- Requested owner, decision, or next action

## Evidence

- Reproduction steps
- Expected result
- Actual result
- Redacted logs, sanitized screenshots, trace IDs, or affected records
- Frequency and scope
- Workarounds tried
- Related tickets, incidents, deployments, or docs

## Redaction Checks

- Remove passwords, tokens, secrets, cookies, and API keys.
- Remove full payment data and unnecessary personal data.
- Use approved secure channels for evidence that cannot be safely redacted.
- Keep customer-facing updates separate from internal escalation notes.

## Owner Routing

- Engineering: suspected bug, regression, performance, or technical blocker
- Product: behavior ambiguity, product gap, or prioritization decision
- Billing: invoice, refund, credit, subscription, or tax issue
- Security: vulnerability, suspicious access, abuse, or data exposure
- Legal/privacy: contractual, compliance, deletion, or data-processing concern
- Success/leadership: strategic account, renewal, churn, or executive attention
