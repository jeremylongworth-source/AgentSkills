# Experiment Design Checklist

- Decision and hypothesis are explicit.
- Experiment tests one main change unless it is intentionally multivariate.
- Primary metric is close enough to the desired behavior.
- Guardrails cover quality, retention, revenue, performance, accessibility, or trust risks.
- Sample, duration, seasonality, novelty effects, and segmentation are considered.
- Instrumentation is tested before launch.
- Stop rules and decision rules are written before results are known.
- Result summary distinguishes statistically measured outcomes, directional signals, and qualitative learning.
