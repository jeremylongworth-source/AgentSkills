- Hypothesis
- Variant/control
- Audience
- Dates
- Sample
- Primary metric
- Guardrails
- Decision rule

- Primary metric movement

- Data quality issues
- Segment notes
- Practical impact

## Decision

- Ship
- Iterate
- Stop
- Extend
- Rerun

- Learning
- Risks
- Next experiment
