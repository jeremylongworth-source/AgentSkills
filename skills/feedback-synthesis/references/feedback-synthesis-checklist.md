# Feedback Synthesis Checklist

## Source Inventory

- Source type: support, sales, interviews, surveys, reviews, analytics, community
- Date range and sample size
- Segment, plan, geography, persona, or lifecycle stage
- Collection method and likely bias
- Relevant product area or workflow

## Coding Dimensions

- Problem or desired outcome
- Current workaround or alternative
- Frequency and severity
- User segment and role
- Business impact
- Emotional intensity
- Evidence confidence

## Synthesis Checks

- Themes preserve the customer's language where useful.
- Claims include counts or evidence only when present.
- Recommendations are separated from evidence.
- Minority but high-severity signals are not averaged away.
- Next steps distinguish research, experiment, design, and implementation work.
