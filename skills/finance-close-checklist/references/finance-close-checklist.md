# Finance Close Checklist

## Workstreams

- Source data readiness
- Bank, payment, and billing reconciliations
- Revenue, deferred revenue, refunds, credits, and discounts
- Expense, vendor, payroll, and accrual inputs
- SaaS, product, marketing, or operational metrics
- Variance commentary and management review
- Reporting package, board package, or investor package
- Approval, publication, and archive steps

## Evidence

- Source reports and dashboard links
- Reconciliation workpapers
- Approval records
- Variance explanations
- Data quality notes
- Signoff checklist

## Risk Flags

- Missing owner or due date
- Unexplained material variance
- Changed definition or source system
- Manual adjustment without review
- External reporting or compliance sensitivity
- Close blocker without mitigation
