# Fundraising Narrative Checklist

## Narrative Inputs

- Stage and target investor type
- Problem, customer, and urgency
- Product and insight
- Market, category, and alternatives
- Traction, revenue, usage, pipeline, or retention
- Team and founder-market fit
- Use of funds and next milestones

## Evidence Checks

- Which claims are proven?
- Which claims are directional?
- Which claims need source review?
- Which assumptions could investors challenge?
- What diligence questions will follow?

## Story Checks

- The narrative is specific to the company.
- The raise timing is clear.
- The story connects traction to future milestones.
- Claims are marked for founder, finance, or counsel review.
