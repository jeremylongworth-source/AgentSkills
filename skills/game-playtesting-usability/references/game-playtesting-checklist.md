# Game Playtesting Checklist

## Planning

- Design question and target player are clear.
- Test type matches the question.
- Build, platform, controls, and session length are defined.
- Success criteria and observation plan are written before the session.
- Consent, recording, privacy, and participant safety are considered.

## Session

- Player is not coached unless help is part of the test design.
- Moderator records behavior, time, failures, confusion, emotion, comments, and workarounds.
- Tasks cover onboarding, core loop, failure/retry, UI, controls, and any risky feature.
- Post-test questions focus on comprehension, motivation, frustration, clarity, and desire to continue.

## Synthesis

- Issues are categorized as bug, usability, balance, content, accessibility, performance, or preference.
- Severity reflects player impact and frequency.
- Recommendations connect evidence to design changes.
- Retest criteria are clear.
