# Growth Strategy Checklist

## Strategy

- ICP, buyer personas, buying committee, pain, urgency, and willingness to pay are explicit.
- Positioning explains why this offer is different, credible, and valuable now.
- Sales motion matches deal size, complexity, and buyer expectations.
- Marketing channels match audience behavior and buying intent.
- Funnel stages, handoffs, and lifecycle definitions are shared across sales and marketing.

## Operating System

- Each initiative has an owner, goal, target metric, budget, start date, and review cadence.
- Leading indicators are defined before lagging revenue metrics arrive.
- CRM and analytics definitions are consistent.
- Experiments have hypotheses, success thresholds, stop rules, and learning capture.
- Compliance, consent, privacy, and brand risk are reviewed before launch.

## Review

- Strategy identifies what not to do.
- Near-term tactics are connected to long-term positioning and pipeline quality.
- Reports distinguish volume, quality, efficiency, velocity, and revenue impact.
