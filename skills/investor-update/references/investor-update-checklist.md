# Investor Update Checklist

## Inputs

- Period and audience
- Stage and fundraising status
- Metrics, traction, revenue, runway, or usage
- Product progress and roadmap
- Customer wins, pipeline, churn, or feedback
- Hiring and team updates
- Risks, blockers, and asks

## Sections

- Opening summary
- Wins and progress
- Metrics
- Challenges and risks
- Next priorities
- Asks
- Review notes

## Review Checks

- Metrics and financials are sourced.
- Forward-looking statements are clearly framed.
- Confidential details are marked for review.
- Asks are specific and actionable.
