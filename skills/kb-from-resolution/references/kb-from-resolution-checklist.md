# KB From Resolution Checklist

## Source Review

- What issue was resolved?
- Who is affected?
- What product area, plan, or environment does it apply to?
- What evidence confirms the resolution?
- What details are customer-specific or internal-only?

## Article Types

- FAQ: short answer to a recurring question
- How-to: planned task with ordered steps
- Troubleshooting: diagnose and resolve an error or symptom
- Known issue: describe status, workaround, and review cadence
- Policy explainer: clarify support scope, billing, or account behavior
- Internal runbook: repeatable support procedure not intended for customers

## Review Checks

- No private customer information is included.
- Steps can be followed by the intended audience.
- The article states prerequisites and expected results.
- Claims match product and policy context.
- Security, privacy, billing, incident, and legal topics are flagged for review.
- Tags and related articles improve findability.
