# Lifecycle Email Checklist

- Audience has valid permission or a documented legal/compliance basis.
- Segment, lifecycle stage, and trigger are explicit.
- Message has one primary job and one primary CTA.
- Journey has entry, exit, suppression, frequency, and fallback rules.
- Sender identity, subject line, physical address, and unsubscribe path meet applicable requirements.
- Inactive or stale contacts are managed deliberately.
- Authentication, bounce handling, complaint monitoring, and domain reputation are considered.
- Metrics include conversion, engagement, unsubscribes, complaints, bounces, pipeline/revenue, retention, and deliverability.
