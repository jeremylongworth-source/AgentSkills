# Marketing Analytics Attribution Checklist

- Every dashboard answers a named decision question.
- Primary conversion events are business-meaningful and tested.
- GA4 key events or ad conversion actions align with campaign goals.
- UTMs are standardized and documented.
- CRM lifecycle stages and opportunity stages are defined consistently.
- Offline conversions and sales outcomes are joined where possible.
- Reports distinguish lead volume from lead quality and revenue.
- Attribution model limitations are disclosed.
- Data QA checks run before strategic conclusions are drawn.
