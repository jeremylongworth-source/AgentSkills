# Media Kit Builder

## Core Workflow

1. Identify creator positioning, audience, platforms, proof points, categories,
   offers, values, and brand-safety boundaries.
2. Structure the media kit with creator snapshot, audience profile, content
   pillars, partnership formats, proof, sample concepts, and contact path.
3. Separate verified metrics from estimates, claims, examples, and placeholders.
4. Add sponsor disclosure, usage-rights, exclusivity, and approval notes.
5. Suggest missing assets: headshot, logos, screenshots, audience data, case
   studies, testimonials, and campaign recaps.
6. Keep copy concise enough for a one-sheet or pitch deck section.

## Safety Rules

- Do not fabricate follower counts, engagement rates, demographics, sponsor
  logos, case studies, testimonials, or conversion results.
- Do not present estimates as verified metrics.
- Require review before sharing externally.

## Deliverable Shape

For media kits, provide:

- Positioning summary
- Audience and platform snapshot
- Content pillars
- Partnership formats
- Proof and metrics table
- Missing asset list
- Review notes
