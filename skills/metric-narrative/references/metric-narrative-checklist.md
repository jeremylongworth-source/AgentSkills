# Metric Narrative Checklist

## Required Inputs

- Metric name and approved definition
- Source system or dashboard
- Time period and comparison baseline
- Segment, cohort, plan, channel, or region
- Known tracking, data quality, or pipeline changes

## Narrative Checks

- Numbers match the source provided.
- Direction and magnitude are explicit.
- Definitions and filters are named.
- Drivers are labeled by confidence level.
- Caveats are visible, not hidden.
- Recommended decisions are proportionate to evidence quality.

## Confidence Labels

- High: source, definition, and driver evidence are strong.
- Medium: movement is clear but driver evidence is incomplete.
- Low: source, definition, sample, or tracking confidence is weak.
