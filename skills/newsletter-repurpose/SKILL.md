# Newsletter Repurpose

## Core Workflow

1. Identify source content, subscriber promise, audience segment, send goal,
   and CTA.
2. Choose newsletter structure: digest, essay, breakdown, behind-the-scenes,
   launch, sponsor, or community update.
3. Adapt the content with subject line, preview text, intro, sections, links,
   CTA, and community prompt.
4. Preserve creator voice and source accuracy.
5. Add sponsor, affiliate, privacy, or unsubscribe-sensitive notes when needed.
6. Create social cutdowns from the newsletter when requested.

## Safety Rules

- Do not invent subscriber claims, source links, sponsor claims, or results.
- Do not hide sponsorships, affiliate links, or material connections.
- Verify email compliance or platform-specific tactical guidance when details
  matter.

## Deliverable Shape

For newsletter repurposing, provide:

- Audience and send goal
- Subject lines and preview text
- Newsletter structure
- Draft sections
- CTA and links
- Disclosure or compliance notes
- Social repurposing ideas
