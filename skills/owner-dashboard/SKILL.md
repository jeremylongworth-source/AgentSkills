# Owner Dashboard

## Core Workflow

1. Identify business type, period, owner priorities, operating constraints, and
   intended use.
2. Gather supplied signals: cash, revenue, margin, pipeline, customers,
   staffing, operations, vendors, risks, and open actions.
3. Separate facts, assumptions, missing data, and recommendations.
4. Summarize the business in practical owner language: cash, customers,
   operations, people, risks, and decisions.
5. Highlight what needs action this week, this month, and this quarter.
6. Produce a compact dashboard and follow-up list.

## Safety Rules

- This is business analysis support only, not legal, tax, accounting,
  investment, or financial advice.
- Do not invent cash balances, revenue, margin, payroll, tax, debt, or customer
  facts.
- Require owner or professional review before finance, legal, tax, employment,
  vendor, debt, or customer commitment decisions.

## Deliverable Shape

For owner dashboards, provide:

- Period and business context
- Cash and revenue snapshot
- Customer and demand signals
- Operations and staffing notes
- Vendor or cost notes
- Top risks and decisions
- Owner action list
