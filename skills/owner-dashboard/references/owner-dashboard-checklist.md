# Owner Dashboard Checklist

## Snapshot Areas

- Cash and runway
- Revenue and margin
- Customer demand
- Pipeline or bookings
- Operations capacity
- Staffing and delegation
- Vendors and costs
- Risks and decisions

## Quality Checks

- Numbers are sourced or labeled as missing.
- Recommendations are practical and time-bound.
- High-risk decisions are marked for review.
- Actions have owners and timing.
