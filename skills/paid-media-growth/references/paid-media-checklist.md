# Paid Media Checklist

- Campaign objective matches business goal and funnel stage.
- Primary conversion is valuable enough for optimization.
- Tracking is tested before launch.
- Audience, message, creative, offer, landing page, and bid strategy are treated as separate hypotheses.
- Creative variants test distinct angles, not tiny cosmetic changes.
- Landing page message matches the ad promise.
- Reports separate spend, reach, CTR, CPC, CVR, CPA, lead quality, pipeline, revenue, CAC, and ROAS.
- Budget changes are tied to learning quality and performance stability.
- Compliance, privacy, claims, and platform policies are reviewed.
