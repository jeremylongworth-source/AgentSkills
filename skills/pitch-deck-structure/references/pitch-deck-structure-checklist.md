# Pitch Deck Structure Checklist

## Common Slide Jobs

- Title and one-line thesis
- Problem and urgency
- Insight or wedge
- Product
- Customer and use case
- Market
- Traction
- Business model
- Go-to-market
- Competition or differentiation
- Team
- Use of funds and milestones
- Ask

## Review Checks

- Every slide has one job.
- Claims are evidence-backed or marked as assumptions.
- The flow matches company stage.
- Weak proof areas are visible before investor use.
