# Pricing Margin Review Checklist

## Inputs

- Current prices
- Costs and margins
- Customer segments
- Capacity or utilization
- Discounts
- Scope or service levels
- Competitor context when available

## Pressure Sources

- Price too low
- Costs rising
- Customer mix
- Scope creep
- Utilization
- Discounting
- Vendor changes

## Options

- Raise price
- Change packages
- Add minimums
- Reduce discounting
- Clarify scope
- Shift customer mix
- Reduce cost
