# Rate Card Builder Checklist

## Inputs

- Platforms and follower/audience metrics
- Average views or engagement where supplied
- Deliverable types
- Production effort
- Usage rights
- Exclusivity
- Campaign term
- Reporting needs

## Pricing Lines

- Base content creation
- Additional cutdowns
- Paid usage or whitelisting
- Exclusivity
- Rush timeline
- Revision rounds
- Reporting

## Review

- Rates are marked draft
- Assumptions are visible
- Missing data is listed
- No legal, tax, or financial advice
