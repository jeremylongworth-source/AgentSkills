# Release Launch Checklist

## Readiness

- Scope, owner, timing, and affected users are clear.
- QA and acceptance gates passed or exceptions are documented.
- Security, privacy, accessibility, performance, analytics, and billing risks are reviewed where relevant.
- Documentation, changelog, help content, and support scripts are ready.
- Marketing, sales, customer success, and support know what is changing.

## Rollout And Rollback

- Release strategy is explicit: flag, cohort, percentage, region, platform, beta, or full launch.
- Rollback path is tested or documented.
- Data migrations and irreversible changes have mitigation plans.
- Feature flags have owners, cleanup plans, and monitoring.

## Monitoring And Response

- Technical health metrics and business metrics are watched.
- Alerts, dashboards, logs, and owners are known.
- Incident channel and escalation path are defined.
- Post-launch review captures results, incidents, customer feedback, and follow-up actions.
