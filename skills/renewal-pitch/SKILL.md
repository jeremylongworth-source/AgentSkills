# Renewal Pitch

## Core Workflow

1. Identify campaign recap, sponsor objective, what worked, audience response,
   remaining opportunity, creator capacity, and proposed next step.
2. Build a renewal angle based on supplied results and observed learnings.
3. Draft follow-up language with recommended package, timeline, and measurement
   plan.
4. Separate proven results from hypotheses for the next campaign.
5. Include rights, disclosure, exclusivity, timeline, and review caveats.
6. Keep the ask specific and easy for the sponsor to evaluate.

## Safety Rules

- Do not invent performance data, sponsor satisfaction, audience comments, or
  conversion results.
- Do not guarantee improved performance or future ROI.
- Do not send renewal outreach or accept terms without approval.

## Deliverable Shape

For renewal pitches, provide:

- Campaign learning summary
- Renewal angle
- Proposed next package
- Follow-up email draft
- Measurement plan
- Assumptions and caveats
- Approval notes
