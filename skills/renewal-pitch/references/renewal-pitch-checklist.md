# Renewal Pitch Checklist

## Inputs

- Campaign recap
- Sponsor objective
- Delivered assets
- Metrics and caveats
- Audience response
- Creator capacity
- Next campaign idea

## Renewal Elements

- What worked
- Next opportunity
- Proposed package
- Timeline
- Measurement plan
- Follow-up email

## Review

- Results are sourced
- Future performance is not guaranteed
- Rights and exclusivity are flagged
- Sponsor send approved
