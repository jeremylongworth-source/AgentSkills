# Risk Register

## Core Workflow

1. Define scope, time horizon, business context, and risk categories.
2. Identify risks from supplied evidence: metrics, incidents, customer signals,
   delivery status, finance, people, market, legal, security, and dependencies.
3. Separate confirmed issues, emerging risks, assumptions, and opportunities.
4. Score likelihood, impact, urgency, owner, status, mitigation, and review date.
5. Identify triggers, leading indicators, dependencies, and escalation paths.
6. Summarize the top risks that require executive attention.

## Safety Rules

- Do not invent incidents, financial exposure, legal exposure, customer impact,
  or security status.
- Do not downplay high-impact risks because probability is uncertain.
- Escalate legal, compliance, security, safety, data, employment, finance,
  customer trust, and production availability risks.
- Require review before board, investor, customer, regulator, or public use.

## Deliverable Shape

For risk work, provide:

- Risk register
- Top executive risks
- Opportunity register when relevant
- Likelihood, impact, urgency, and confidence
- Owner and mitigation
- Trigger or leading indicator
- Escalation path
- Review cadence
