# Risk Register Checklist

## Risk Categories

- Strategic
- Financial
- Customer
- Product
- Delivery
- People
- Legal and compliance
- Security and privacy
- Reputation
- Vendor or dependency

## Register Fields

- Risk statement
- Evidence or signal
- Likelihood
- Impact
- Urgency
- Confidence
- Owner
- Mitigation
- Trigger or leading indicator
- Escalation path
- Review date

## Review Checks

- High-impact risks are visible even when probability is uncertain.
- Risks and issues are labeled separately.
- Owners and mitigations are specific.
- External or board-facing language is marked for review.
