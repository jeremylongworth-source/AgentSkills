# Pipeline Management Checklist

- Pipeline stages match observable buyer/seller milestones.
- Entry and exit criteria are written for each stage.
- Required fields support qualification, forecasting, handoff, and analysis.
- Stale opportunities, missing next steps, slipped close dates, and duplicate records are visible.
- Stage conversion, velocity, win rate, deal size, source, forecast category, and loss reasons are tracked.
- Forecast calls inspect evidence, risk, mutual plan, buyer process, and procurement/legal dependencies.
- Closed-lost reasons are specific enough to improve strategy.
- Post-purchase handoff supports onboarding, renewal, expansion, and referrals.
