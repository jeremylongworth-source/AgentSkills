# Prospecting Outreach Checklist

- ICP and disqualification criteria are explicit.
- Each segment has a relevant pain, trigger, offer, and proof point.
- Contact data source and permission basis are documented.
- Subject lines and headers are accurate.
- Commercial email includes required sender identity, postal address where required, and opt-out path.
- Sequence has stop rules for replies, opt-outs, hard bounces, and disqualification.
- CTA asks for one clear next action.
- Metrics track positive replies, meetings, qualified pipeline, unsubscribes, complaints, and revenue.
