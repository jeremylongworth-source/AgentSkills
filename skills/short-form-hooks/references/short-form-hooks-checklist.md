# Short-Form Hooks Checklist

## Hook Angles

- Curiosity
- Problem
- Mistake
- Myth
- Contrarian take
- Transformation
- Proof
- Story moment
- Tutorial promise
- Audience identity

## Review

- Truthful
- Specific
- Fast to understand
- Matches creator voice
- Has a payoff
- Avoids unsupported claims
- Avoids shame or manipulation

## Output

- Hook
- First beat
- Visual idea
- Payoff
- Test note
