# Sponsorship Package

## Core Workflow

1. Identify creator capacity, audience, platform mix, sponsor goal, campaign
   length, deliverable types, and review constraints.
2. Build package options around outcomes and deliverables, not vague exposure.
3. Define deliverables, review rounds, timeline, usage-rights assumptions,
   reporting, exclusions, and add-ons for each package.
4. Keep pricing separate unless the user provides rates or asks for a rate-card
   draft.
5. Flag exclusivity, whitelisting, paid usage, affiliate, giveaway, and product
   claim questions.
6. Recommend a simple package structure that avoids overpromising performance.

## Safety Rules

- Do not promise reach, sales, conversions, virality, or sponsor acceptance.
- Do not invent deliverable capacity or platform performance.
- Do not provide legal, tax, or financial advice.

## Deliverable Shape

For sponsorship packages, provide:

- Package table
- Deliverables and timeline
- Review and approval assumptions
- Rights and exclusivity notes
- Reporting included
- Add-ons and exclusions
- Open questions
