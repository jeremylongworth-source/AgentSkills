# Synthetic Character Bible Checklist

## Character Definition

- Purpose and audience
- Fictional, mascot, virtual influencer, host, or assistant role
- Relationship to the real creator or brand
- Voice, tone, vocabulary, and catchphrases
- Visual direction and continuity markers
- Content lanes and restricted topics

## Trust and Rights Checks

- Disclosure and authenticity rules
- No real-person impersonation without rights review
- No protected characters, living artists, logos, or private likenesses without
  permission review
- Voice, likeness, and consent boundaries
- Sponsor, affiliate, and product-claim restrictions
- AI provenance and source-asset notes

## Review Gates

- Public launch
- Sponsored use
- Sensitive topics
- Claims of lived experience, expertise, results, or identity
- Audience interaction
- Platform-specific distribution
- Corrections or public backlash

## Output Checks

- Fictional facts are separated from real creator or sponsor facts.
- Prohibited claims are explicit.
- Human review owner is named.
- No account creation, publication, or impersonation action is implied.
