# Thumbnail Title Brief Checklist

## Title

- Specific promise
- Clear audience
- Curiosity without deception
- Strong noun/verb
- Matches payoff
- Avoids unsupported claims

## Thumbnail

- Focal subject
- Visual contrast
- Minimal text
- Emotion or tension
- Proof element
- Composition notes
- Avoid list

## Pairing

- One clear idea
- No duplicate wording if visual says it
- Testable variants
