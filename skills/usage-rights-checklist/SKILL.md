# Usage Rights Checklist

## Core Workflow

1. Identify content assets, sponsor use case, channels, term, territory,
   exclusivity, paid usage, whitelisting, edits, approvals, and reporting.
2. Convert rights questions into a checklist for creator, manager, sponsor, or
   counsel review.
3. Separate content creation deliverables from usage, licensing, paid media, and
   exclusivity rights.
4. Flag missing contract terms, ambiguous permissions, and high-risk clauses.
5. Provide negotiation questions without giving legal advice.
6. Recommend professional review before signing or accepting rights terms.

## Safety Rules

- Do not provide legal advice or interpret enforceability.
- Do not approve contracts, licenses, exclusivity, or usage rights.
- Do not assume the creator owns third-party music, footage, images, logos, or
  likenesses.

## Deliverable Shape

For usage-rights reviews, provide:

- Rights summary
- Checklist table
- Risk flags
- Missing terms
- Negotiation questions
- Review owner
- Professional review note
