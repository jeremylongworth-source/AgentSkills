# Usage Rights Checklist

## Rights Questions

- Which assets are covered?
- Where can the sponsor use them?
- Is paid media usage included?
- Is whitelisting or boosting included?
- What is the term length?
- What territories are included?
- Are edits or derivatives allowed?
- Is exclusivity required?
- Who approves changes?

## Risk Flags

- Perpetual rights
- Broad exclusivity
- Unlimited paid usage
- Undefined derivatives
- Missing approval process
- Third-party assets

## Review

- Not legal advice
- Counsel or manager review needed
- Creator approval required before accepting
