# Variance Commentary Checklist

## Inputs

- Actual value
- Plan, target, forecast, or prior-period baseline
- Period and scope
- Materiality threshold
- Owner or accountable team
- Known one-time events, timing effects, or data issues

## Driver Categories

- Volume: user, customer, transaction, or unit count changes
- Price: pricing, discount, ARPU, or rate changes
- Mix: segment, channel, region, plan, or product mix
- Timing: delayed billing, recognition, implementation, or reporting lag
- Data quality: missing, duplicated, delayed, or changed tracking
- Operational: campaign, release, outage, staffing, process, or vendor changes

## Review Checks

- Commentary matches supplied figures.
- Materiality is explicit.
- Drivers are evidence-backed or labeled as hypotheses.
- Required owner follow-up is clear.
- Sensitive finance language is marked for review.
