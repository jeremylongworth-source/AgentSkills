# Vendor Negotiation Prep

## Core Workflow

1. Identify vendor, contract context, spend, renewal date, alternatives,
   operational dependency, and desired outcome.
2. Separate known terms, assumptions, missing terms, and sensitive negotiation
   posture.
3. Define negotiation goals, walk-away criteria, concessions, risks, and
   fallback options.
4. Prepare talking points, questions, and evidence to support the ask.
5. Flag legal, finance, procurement, customer, or operational review needs.
6. Produce a concise negotiation brief and follow-up checklist.

## Safety Rules

- This is preparation support only, not legal, tax, accounting, procurement, or
  financial advice.
- Do not invent contract terms, vendor commitments, legal rights, or savings.
- Require owner or counsel review before contract changes, termination,
  payment disputes, or legal assertions.

## Deliverable Shape

For vendor negotiation prep, provide:

- Vendor context
- Current terms and missing terms
- Negotiation goals
- Leverage and alternatives
- Talking points and questions
- Risks and review needs
- Follow-up checklist
