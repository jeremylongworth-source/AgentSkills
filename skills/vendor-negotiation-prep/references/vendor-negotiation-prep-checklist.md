# Vendor Negotiation Prep Checklist

## Inputs

- Vendor and service
- Current spend
- Contract terms
- Renewal date
- Alternatives
- Operational dependency
- Desired outcome

## Negotiation Prep

- Goals
- Walk-away criteria
- Concessions
- Evidence
- Questions
- Fallback option
- Review owner

## Review Flags

- Contract change
- Termination
- Payment dispute
- Legal assertion
- Customer impact
- Operational risk
