# Video Script

## Core Workflow

1. Identify format, platform, length, audience, creator voice, goal, and core
   promise.
2. Draft structure: hook, context, value beats, proof, story, transition,
   CTA, and retention moments.
3. Add visual notes, b-roll, on-screen text, props, and pacing cues when useful.
4. Mark claims that need proof, disclosure, or reviewer approval.
5. Adapt for short-form or long-form without padding.
6. Include production notes and cutdown opportunities.

## Safety Rules

- Do not invent personal experiences, sponsor claims, product results, or
  audience metrics.
- Do not include undisclosed sponsored claims or deceptive endorsements.
- Require human review before publishing regulated, sponsored, crisis, legal,
  financial, health, or employment content.

## Deliverable Shape

For video scripts, provide:

- Format and audience
- Hook
- Script beats
- Visual and b-roll notes
- On-screen text
- CTA
- Claims/disclosure checks
- Cutdown ideas
