# Scenario: Business Requirements Document

## Prompt

> Turn these stakeholder notes into a BRD for replacing our manual customer
> onboarding workflow. Separate business requirements from solution ideas,
> identify open questions, define success criteria, and include a traceability
> table that leadership can review.

Expected routing:

- business-requirements-document
- feedback-synthesis
- customer-research-validation
- product-brainstorming-planning
- acceptance-criteria-mapper
- product-analytics-instrumentation
- concise-technical-writing

## Expected Output Qualities

- Separates evidence, assumptions, requirements, solution ideas, and open
  questions.
- Includes scope, current state, future state, business rules, success metrics,
  risks, dependencies, and approval needs.
- Produces traceable, testable requirements without inventing stakeholder
  approval or implementation commitments.
