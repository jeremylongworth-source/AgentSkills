# Scenario: Guided Requirements Interview

## Prompt

> Grill me on this idea until you have enough to draft a BRD. I want to replace
> a messy spreadsheet-based onboarding workflow, but I only have a rough goal
> and a few pain points right now.

Expected routing:

- guided-requirements-interview
- business-requirements-document
- product-brainstorming-planning
- acceptance-criteria-mapper
- concise-technical-writing

## Expected Output Qualities

- Starts with a short working frame instead of a long questionnaire.
- Asks one to three focused questions at a time.
- Explains what each question round will clarify.
- Separates confirmed facts, assumptions, open questions, and decisions.
- Stops when enough information exists for a useful BRD draft.
